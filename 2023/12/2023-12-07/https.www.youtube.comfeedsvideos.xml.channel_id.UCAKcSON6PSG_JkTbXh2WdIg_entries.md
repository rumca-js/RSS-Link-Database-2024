Jason Isbell with Amanda Shires and Sadler Vaden performing a trio version of "If We Were Vampires" for The Current in the Forum at Minnesota Public Radio in 2018. The song struck a powerful chord with The Current's listeners; in fact, not long before this performance was recorded, the song was voted by listeners to the No. 1 spot in The Current's Top 89 of 2017.
