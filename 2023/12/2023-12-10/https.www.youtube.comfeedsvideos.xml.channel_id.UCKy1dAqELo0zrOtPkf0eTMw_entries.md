Holstin is a pixel-based psychological survival horror game developed by Sonka. Set in the 90's in a Polish town, players will explore an eerie town, fight unexplainable monsters, and interrogate the locals to seek the truth. Holstin centers around the notion of changing perspectives switching from a classic top-down view to a more modern over-the-shoulder third-person perspective during combat. Holstin is coming to PlayStation, Xbox, Nintendo Switch, and PC in 2024 with a demo on PC available now.
