We all have a tendency to talk about and (rightly) criticize developers and publishers in the industry who chase trends in the worst, most misguided way possible, but at the same time, there are also plenty of trends in the industry that more often than not lead to good things. 

2023 in and of itself has seen plenty of these, from practices that suddenly grew in popularity this year to those that had steadily been gathering momentum for some time before reaching their peak over the course of the last few months. 

Here, that’s exactly what we’re here to talk about, as we take a look at a few gaming trends of 2023 that we only have good things to say about.
