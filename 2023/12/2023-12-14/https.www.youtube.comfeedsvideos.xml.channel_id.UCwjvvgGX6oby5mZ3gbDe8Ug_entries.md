Over the past seven to eight years, the European countries had been quite moderate toward the CCP regime even as relations between Beijing and their ally, the US, had become increasingly strained. Recently, the Beijing government has made frequent friendly gestures to the European Union. On December 1, 2023, China unilaterally introduced a two-week visa-free travel policy for France, Germany, Spain, Italy, and the Netherlands. Beijing also lifted sanctions against Lithuania for its proximity to Taiwan.
