If you want to know what it's like to be at the South Pole in winter, check out northeastern China. Some cities in this region have seen record lows of - 48 Celsius or -54.4°F. 
At 23:59 on December 18 when the temperature was -12 Celsius or 10.4°F, a magnitude 6.2 earthquake struck a county in Gansu Province. The depth of the quake was 10 kilometers.
