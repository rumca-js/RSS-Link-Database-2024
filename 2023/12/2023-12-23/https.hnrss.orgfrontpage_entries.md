## Show HN: 4d-Modeller – R library to make Bayesian spatiotemporal modeling easy
 - [https://4dmodeller.github.io/fdmr](https://4dmodeller.github.io/fdmr)
 - RSS feed: https://hnrss.org/frontpage
 - date published: 2023-12-23T09:46:13+00:00

<p>Hi, I've been working with other colleagues at University of Bristol for the last year to build an R-package that makes bayesian spatio-temporal modeling easy.
