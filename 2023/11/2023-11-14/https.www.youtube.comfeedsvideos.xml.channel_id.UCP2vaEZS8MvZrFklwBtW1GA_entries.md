Mitered subway tile shower niche Part 1
Mitered shower niches begin with beveled tiles - I start by beveling the tiles on the bottom of the niche. Normally my bevel is 1/16 inch to 1/32 inch from the finished edge. This eliminates chipping and keeps the miter size the same as the grout joint
