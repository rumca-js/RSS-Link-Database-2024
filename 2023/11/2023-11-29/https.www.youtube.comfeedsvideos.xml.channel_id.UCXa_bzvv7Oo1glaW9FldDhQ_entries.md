Years after first announcing it, the developer has finally announced a release date for Dragon's Dogma 2. It's out on March 22nd, 2024, for Xbox Series X/S, PS5 and PC, with pre-orders now available.

Thanks to the game's recent showcase, we know much more about the upcoming action RPG. There's a new Vocation, Trickster, and some cool new enemies like Dullahan. 

There is a ton to look forward to, including the new Pawn Specializations, improved chatter among Pawns, a new location and more.
