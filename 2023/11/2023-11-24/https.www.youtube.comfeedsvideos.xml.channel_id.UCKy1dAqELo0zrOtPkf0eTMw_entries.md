Merlin Trials are short puzzles that can be found throughout the various Regions in Hogwarts Legacy's open-world. Merlin Trials are arguably the most lucrative of Exploration Challenges, as completing them can earn you additional Gear Inventory Slots, which will save you the trouble of having to frequently sell or destroy the loot you've collected. As you learn the Alohomora unlocking spell from the groundskeeper, Mr. Moon, you’ll find out about Demiguise Statues - small statues of creatures holding glowing moons that can be obtained only when they shimmer at night.
