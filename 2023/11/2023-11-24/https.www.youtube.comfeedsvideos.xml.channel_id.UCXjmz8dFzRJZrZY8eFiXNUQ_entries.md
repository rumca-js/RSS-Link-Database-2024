When Brooklyn Nine Nine was first announced as a new Fox Sitcom, most people assumed this "funny cop show" was doomed to fail.  Hot off of Saturday Night Live, Andy Samberg and crew set out to create something different than expected.  After 8 seasons, and 1 almost-cancellation, Brooklyn Nine Nine has cemented itself as one of the best network sitcoms of the last decade.  Though how exactly did Brooklyn Nine Nine crush our expectations and succeed when nobody thought it would?
