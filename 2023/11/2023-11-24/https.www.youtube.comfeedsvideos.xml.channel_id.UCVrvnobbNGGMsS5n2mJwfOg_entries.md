I was recently invited to Paris, I decided to make it a memorable trip by bringing along my dad for some quality father-son time. We hopped on the Eurostar from London, reaching Paris in just over two hours. The main reason for the trip was to learn about new tech innovation at the Huawei Connect Europe 2023 event. There were insightful talks and panels, and I got to participate in the 'Better Together, Europe Go Green Forum.' There, I met Gary from the Tech4All division. One of the projects he shared was the Smart Whale Sounds initiative in Ireland. Sound pollution from increased marine traffic poses a significant threat to life in the Irish Sea so in collaboration with Ocean Research and Conservation Ireland, Tech4All have deployed autonomous underwater microphones on a solar-powered buoy.
