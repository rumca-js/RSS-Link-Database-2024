In the meantime, my mom has been on my case for ages, insisting I make this unreleased demo track available as a standalone video on the channel. The track was a bit too much in every sense to be included on the album :)
My mom has also been telling me I should play more music in the technical videos, which turned into a bit of a meme, with people saying she’s right about that. We’ll see if she’s right about this one too. “I’m always right,” she likes to remind me often…
This video is from 2011, and it shows the house in the Swedish forest where I made the first Wintergatan songs.
