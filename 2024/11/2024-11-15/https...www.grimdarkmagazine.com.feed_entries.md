<p>In 2050, the world collapsed. Monstrous beings known as Titans appeared and destroyed the Earth. Immortal and impossibly indestructible, they laid waste to cities and civilization. Their destruction had brought forth the apocalypse. History repeats with Spartans rescuing humankind. A new age dawns and Spartans arise as ruthless gods. An original twist of alternate history and reimagined Greek mythology collide in Blood of Hercules by Jasmine Mas. Even by post-apocalyptic standards, Alexis Hert is dirt poor. She has nothing to her name, save the cardboard shelter she and her brother live in. Besides school, she spends her days stealing food vouchers and trying not to smell homeless. Both are equally futile endeavors. Her one hope is to score high enough on the Spartan merit test to secure a better life for her small family. As per usual, fate stomps on her humble dreams.
