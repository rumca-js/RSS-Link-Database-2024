## How Safe Are Food Preservatives, Really?
 - [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lmDJIDeO1Zs](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lmDJIDeO1Zs)
 - RSS feed: $source
 - date published: 2024-11-08T18:00:36+00:00

You may have seen some unpronounceable words or weird abbreviations in your food's ingredient lists before, but have you ever wondered why that stuff gets added into your snacks? It's not just for fun, those preservatives are all there to keep you and your food safe. Let's talk about a few of the most common preservatives, what they do, and why you probably don't need to worry about them.
