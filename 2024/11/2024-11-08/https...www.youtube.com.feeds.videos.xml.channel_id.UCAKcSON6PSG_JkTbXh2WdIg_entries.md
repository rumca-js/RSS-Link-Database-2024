Singer-songwriter Michael Kiwanuka has been a busy guy. He’s been on a dual-headlining tour with Brittany Howard, and this week, he’s getting ready to release his next album, "Small Changes" (out November 15 on @geffenrecords)

While he was in St. Paul as part of his tour with Brittany Howard, Michael Kiwanuka walked over to The Current to chat with Bill DeVille about the upcoming album, including what it was like to work with producers Inflo and Danger Mouse — and to work with esteemed Minneapolis musician and producer Jimmy Jam. Kiwanuka also describes a recent vinyl find he loves! Watch the full interview above.
