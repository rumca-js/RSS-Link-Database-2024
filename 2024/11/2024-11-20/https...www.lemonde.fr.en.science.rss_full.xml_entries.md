Brain implants, headbands and headphones fitted with electrodes: Brain reading and stimulation, once focused solely on therapeutic research, are now edging closer to general use, without much perspective or proof of effectiveness. The aim is no longer simply to treat and repair, but potentially to enhance brain function.
