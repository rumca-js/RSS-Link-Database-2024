Lightning Talk: Welcome to Bristol - Kevlin Henny - ACCU 2024
---

Kevlin Henny provides an engaging and informal guide to Bristol, tailored for ACCU conference attendees. Drawing on his long-term residency since the 1980s, he offers practical advice and highlights key areas and activities within walking distance of the venue. He mentions popular food spots, bars, and unique places like vinyl record stores, guitar shops, and cultural sites such as Stokes Croft and the Clifton Suspension Bridge. He also humorously addresses common queries, such as places to smoke and where to find Warhammer merchandise, making the talk both informative and entertaining.
