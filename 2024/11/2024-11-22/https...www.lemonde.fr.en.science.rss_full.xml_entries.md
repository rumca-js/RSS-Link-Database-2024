## Plants rise to the challenge of adapting to life in the heart of cities
 - [https://www.lemonde.fr/en/science/article/2024/11/22/plants-rise-to-the-challenge-of-adapting-to-life-in-the-heart-of-cities_6733760_10.html](https://www.lemonde.fr/en/science/article/2024/11/22/plants-rise-to-the-challenge-of-adapting-to-life-in-the-heart-of-cities_6733760_10.html)
 - RSS feed: $source
 - date published: 2024-11-22T18:16:38.560707+00:00

A French team has measured the morphological changes in four herbaceous plants growing in the eastern city of Strasbourg, confronted with urban heat islands, building density and repeated mowing. They found that some of these species can adapt.
