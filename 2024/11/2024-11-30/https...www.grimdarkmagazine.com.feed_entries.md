<p>Last Updated on December 1, 2024 Joe Ledger returns in Burn to Shine, the fourth installment in author Jonathan Maberry’s Rogue Team International series. Never a stranger to bioterrorism, Joe Ledger takes on an all-new adventure to combat a mysterious organization infiltrating top-secret biological weapons research facilities around the globe. The stakes have never been higher for Joe Ledger and company, but will they be able to unravel this conspiracy before it’s too late? Jonathan Maberry throws us immediately into the action, using short chapters to switch rapidly among locations as we learn about human disease bombs who are purposely infecting large groups of people in public settings. Although the abrupt chapters feel rather disorienting at first, the narrative style effectively conveys the far-reaching scope of the bioterrorism threat.
