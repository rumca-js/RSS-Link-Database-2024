The Illrigger was first created by MCDM’s Matt Colville, and this infernal-flavored Paladin was recently reworked for The Illrigger Revised. If you’re willing to spend $14.99 on a third-party supplement, you’ll get a whole new class to bring to your gaming table – but I’d probably advise against it.</p>
