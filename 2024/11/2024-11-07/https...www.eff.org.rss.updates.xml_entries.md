<p>X’s lawsuit against the nonprofit Center for Countering Digital Hate is intended to stifle criticism and punish the organization for its reports criticizing the platform’s content moderation practices, and a previous ruling dismissing the lawsuit should be affirmed, EFF and multiple organizations argued <a href="https://www.eff.org/document/x-v-ccdh-eff-aclu-aclu-norcal-and-knight-institute-amicus-brief">in a brief filed</a> this fall. </p>
