There’s something fundamentally profound about alien life that has kept their looming threat at the top of global pop culture for over a hundred years. It isn’t just movies, TV, books, and art that have propagated aliens’ notoriety.

No, video games have been getting in on the act since their origin too. There are tons of games available where you fight against (and sometimes for) interstellar life, and their popularity is likely down to interstellar life’s enigmatic nature. What do aliens look like? 

Will they be more technologically advanced than us? Do they come in peace? The games included here put extra-terrestrial forces under the microscope; this selection perhaps answering some of humanity’s pervading questions through their creativity, innovativeness, and (most often) their eagerness for violence and destruction.
