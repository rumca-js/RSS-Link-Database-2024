## Phase segregation and nanoconfined fluid O<sub>2</sub> in a lithium-rich oxide cathode

<p>Nature Materials, Published online: 13 May 2024; <a href="https://www.nature.com/articles/s41563-024-01873-5">doi:10.1038/s41563-024-01873-5</a></p>Lithium-rich oxygen-redox cathodes demonstrate high capacities, but lose energy density when cycled, showing cation disordering and formation of nanovoids and bulk molecular O2.
