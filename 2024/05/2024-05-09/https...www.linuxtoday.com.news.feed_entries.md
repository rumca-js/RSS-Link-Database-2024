## Mixxx 2.4.1 DJ App Improves Support for Denon, Pioneer, and Traktor Controllers
 - [https://www.linuxtoday.com/news/mixxx-2-4-1-dj-app-improves-support-for-denon-pioneer-and-traktor-controllers](https://www.linuxtoday.com/news/mixxx-2-4-1-dj-app-improves-support-for-denon-pioneer-and-traktor-controllers)
 - RSS feed: https://www.linuxtoday.com/news/feed
 - date published: 2024-05-09T17:29:30+00:00

<p>Mixxx 2.4.1 is the first point release in the latest Mixxx 2.4 stable series improving support for the Behringer DDM4000, Behringer BCR2000, Denon DJ MC6000MK2, Denon DJ MC7000, Hercules Inpulse 200, Pioneer CDJ, Pioneer DDJ-FLX4, Traktor Kontrol F1, Traktor S2 Mk1, and Traktor S3 controllers, as well as for the Nintendo Wii remote.</p>
