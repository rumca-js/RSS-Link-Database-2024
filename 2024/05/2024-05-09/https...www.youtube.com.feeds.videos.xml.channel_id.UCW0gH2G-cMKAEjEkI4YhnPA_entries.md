Peter Jackson, Fran Walsh, Philippa Boyens, and Andy Serkis are returning to Middle-earth!  Just announced today, a new film set in the Lord of the Rings universe will release in 2026 - Hunt for Gollum.  Serkis will return to play Gollum as well as direct.  Jackson, Walsh, & Boyens are producing.  The script is currently being worked on by Walsh and Boyens as well as War of the Rohirrim writing duo Phoebe Gittins and Arty Papageorgiou.
