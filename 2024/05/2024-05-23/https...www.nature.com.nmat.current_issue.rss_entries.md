## Clamping enables enhanced electromechanical responses in antiferroelectric thin films
 - [https://www.nature.com/articles/s41563-024-01907-y](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41563-024-01907-y)
 - RSS feed: https://www.nature.com/nmat/current_issue/rss
 - date published: 2024-05-23T12:18:36.520073+00:00

<p>Nature Materials, Published online: 23 May 2024; <a href="https://www.nature.com/articles/s41563-024-01907-y">doi:10.1038/s41563-024-01907-y</a></p>Here, the authors observe that in thin films of antiferroelectric PbZrO3, substrate clamping enhances the electromechanical response, with expansion purely in the out-of-plane direction, achieving 1.7% strain for 100-nm-thick films.
