When I looked at the genders of dance callers at large contra dance
events </span>

<a href="https://www.jefftk.com/p/contra-caller-gender-ii">several years
ago</a> there was an interesting pattern where events were more likely
to book a man and a woman than you'd expect by chance.  With more
years worth of data to look at, I thought it was worth checking if
this was still the case.
