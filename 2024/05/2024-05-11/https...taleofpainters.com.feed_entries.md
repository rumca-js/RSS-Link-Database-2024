## Review: Chronicle Cards Techlon Tiger Drybrushes set
 - [https://taleofpainters.com/2024/05/review-chronicle-cards-techlon-tiger-drybrushes-set](https://taleofpainters.com/2024/05/review-chronicle-cards-techlon-tiger-drybrushes-set)

 - date published: 2024-05-11T13:00:00+00:00

<p>Last year Chronicle Cards brought their third brush set to life on Kickstarter. Following the all-round wolf bristle brushes and fine Kolinsky sable brushes, they've now created a set of drybrushes made from synthetic hair. The set has now seen a general release in Chronicle Cards' online shop, and I've had the opportunity to thoroughly test the Techlon brushes. In this review, I'll explain why I consider the Techlon Tiger Drybrush Set to be one of the best on the market.</p>
