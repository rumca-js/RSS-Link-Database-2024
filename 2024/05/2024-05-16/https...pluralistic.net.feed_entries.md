Utah's getting some of America's best broadband (permalink) Residents of 21 cities in Utah have access to some of the fastest, most competitively priced broadband in the country, at speeds up to 10gb/s and prices as low as $75/month. It's uncapped, and the connections are symmetrical: perfect for uploading and downloading. And it's all thanks to the government. This broadband service is, of course, delivered via fiber optic cable. Of course it is.
