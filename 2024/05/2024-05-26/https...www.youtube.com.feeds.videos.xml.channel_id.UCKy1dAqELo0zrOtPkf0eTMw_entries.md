Sanctua is an action-adventure co-op game developed by Choose Red. Developed in Unreal Engine 5, players must work together to survive the deadly perils of a procedurally generated tomb. Offering a top-down camera that offers a clear overview of the surroundings to make communication and coordination possible to endure the deadly traps, cursed wildlife, and the ancient guardian that stalks the ever-shifting ruins. Sanctua is launching on PC in Q2 2025 for Steam and Epic Games Store.
