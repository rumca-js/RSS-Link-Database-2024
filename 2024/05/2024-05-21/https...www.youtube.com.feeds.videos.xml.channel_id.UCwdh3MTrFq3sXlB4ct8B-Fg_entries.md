Skaven are the spawn of the malevolent deity known as the Great Horned Rat. Their ruling Great Clans specialise in everything from creating foul monstrosities to spreading plagues. In this guide, Adam will show you how to quickly paint Clanrats in the colours of five different Skaven Great Clans, in order to get your swarm on the tabletop in no time at all!
