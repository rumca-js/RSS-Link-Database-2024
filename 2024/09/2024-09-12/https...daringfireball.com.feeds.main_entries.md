## FDA Grants Approval to AirPods Pro 2 for Use as Hearing Aids
 - [https://techcrunch.com/2024/09/12/apple-airpods-pro-granted-fda-approval-to-serve-as-hearing-aids/?guccounter=1](https://techcrunch.com/2024/09/12/apple-airpods-pro-granted-fda-approval-to-serve-as-hearing-aids/?guccounter=1)
 - RSS feed: https://daringfireball.com/feeds/main
 - date published: 2024-09-12T23:34:41+00:00


<p>Brian Heater, reporting for TechCrunch:</p>

<blockquote>
  <p>The iPhone 16 took center stage at Apple’s “It’s Glowtime” event,
but the most interesting tidbit came from a different line
entirely. Indeed, among a sea of new hardware came an intriguing
software update to one already on the market: the AirPods Pro 2.</p>

<p>Apple announced that its most premium earbuds would double as an
over-the-counter hearing aid, courtesy of a software update,
pending approval from the U.S. Food and Drug Administration.</p>

<p><a href="https://www.fda.gov/news-events/press-announcements/fda-authorizes-first-over-counter-hearing-aid-software">The FDA on Thursday announced</a> that it has granted what it
calls “the first over-the-counter (OTC) hearing aid software
device, Hearing Aid Feature.” Specifically, it has approved the
software update that enables that functionality.</p>
</blockquote>
