Guangzhou has officially announced that the unemployed will reach 900,000 in 2024. Stores will close, and factories will lay off employees and reduce positions, making more and more workers who stay in Guangdong feel confused, depressed and anxious. Netizens bluntly said that the 900,000 unemployed population is a conservative estimate.
