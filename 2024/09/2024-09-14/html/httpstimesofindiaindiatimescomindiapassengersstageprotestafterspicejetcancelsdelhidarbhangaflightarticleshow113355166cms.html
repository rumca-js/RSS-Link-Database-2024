[1145754] Passengers stage protest after SpiceJet cancels Delhi-Darbhanga flight

https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/rssfeedstopstories.cms
2024-09-14 18:26:39.230520+00:00
Disgruntled passengers staged a protest at Delhi airport after SpiceJet cancelled its Delhi to Darbhanga flight on Saturday due to unforeseen operational reasons. The airline apologized for the inconvenience and provided affected passengers with options of either a full refund or booking an alternative flight the next day, subject to availability.
