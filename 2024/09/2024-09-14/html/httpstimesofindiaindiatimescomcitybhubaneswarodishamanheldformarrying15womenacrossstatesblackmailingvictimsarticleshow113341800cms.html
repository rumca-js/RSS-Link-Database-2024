Biranchi Narayan Nath, aged 43 from Angul district, was apprehended by the state crime branch for allegedly marrying over a dozen women across multiple states and blackmailing them using objectionable videos. Nath posed as various central government officers on matrimonial sites to deceive unmarried women, widows, and divorcees for financial and sexual exploitation.
