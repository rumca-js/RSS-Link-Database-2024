[1145621] 2nd Iran-China Watercolor Exchange Exhibition held in Tehran

https://www.tehrantimes.com/rss
2024-09-14 16:48:23+00:00
TEHRAN-The second Iran-China Watercolor Exchange Exhibition was held at the Art Garden Pavilion (Kushk Art Garden) in Tehran from September 7 to 13 and its closing ceremony was held at the Tabi'at Bridge (the bridge of nature).
