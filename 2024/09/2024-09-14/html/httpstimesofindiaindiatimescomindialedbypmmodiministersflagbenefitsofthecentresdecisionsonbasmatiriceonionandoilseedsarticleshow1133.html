[1145291] Led by PM Modi, ministers flag benefits of the Centre's decisions on Basmati rice, onion and oilseeds

https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/rssfeedstopstories.cms
2024-09-14 16:16:41.516732+00:00
The Indian government has removed the minimum export price for onions and Basmati rice and raised import duties on edible oils. Prime Minister Modi emphasized that these measures will boost exports, increase farmers' incomes, and create more jobs in rural areas. Ministers Shah and Chouhan echoed these benefits for various crops and oilseeds.
