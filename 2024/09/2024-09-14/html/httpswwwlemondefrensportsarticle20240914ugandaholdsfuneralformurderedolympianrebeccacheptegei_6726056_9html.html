[1145445] Uganda holds funeral for murdered Olympian Rebecca Cheptegei

https://www.lemonde.fr/en/rss/une.xml
2024-09-14 17:14:08.396576+00:00
The 33-year-old long-distance runner, who had died after being set on fire by her partner, was given a military funeral, including a gun salute. Her death has thrown a spotlight on what activists have called a femicide epidemic.
