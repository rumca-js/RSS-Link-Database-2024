Jasprit Bumrah recently called himself as India's fittest cricketer over Virat Kohli and Ravindra Jadeja, emphasizing the physical toll of fast bowling. Bumrah is gearing up for the India vs Bangladesh Test series beginning September 19 in Chennai, actively participating in a four-day training camp with his teammates to prepare for the series.
