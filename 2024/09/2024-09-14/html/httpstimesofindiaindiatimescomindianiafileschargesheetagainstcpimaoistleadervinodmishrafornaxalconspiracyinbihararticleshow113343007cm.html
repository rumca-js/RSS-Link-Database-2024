[1143438] NIA files chargesheet against CPI (M) leader Vinod Mishra

https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/rssfeedstopstories.cms
2024-09-14 07:18:50.770196+00:00
The National Investigation Agency (NIA) has filed a supplementary chargesheet against Vinod Mishra for his involvement in reviving the banned CPI (Maoist) in Bihar's Magadh region. Mishra provided shelter and logistical support to Maoist leaders. This action follows the arrest of two top CPI (Maoist) leaders in August 2023, leading to further investigations.
