Kepler Wessels stands out as the only cricketer to score over 1000 runs and centuries in Test matches for two nations: Australia and South Africa. Beginning his career with Australia, he later captained South Africa post-isolation, leading them in their first World Cup appearance since the 1970s and achieving remarkable feats. Throughout his Test career, Wessels scored six centuries and 15 half-centuries. In his ODI career, which spanned 109 matches, he scored one century and 26 fifties.
