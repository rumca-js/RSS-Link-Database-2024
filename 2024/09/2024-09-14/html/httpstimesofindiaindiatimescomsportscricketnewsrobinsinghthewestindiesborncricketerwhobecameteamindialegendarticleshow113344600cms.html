Robin Singh from Trinidad and Tobago became an important figure in Indian cricket. Despite a brief Test career, he excelled in ODIs with notable batting and bowling contributions. Post-retirement, he transitioned to coaching roles that improved India's fielding standards and contributed to successes in the Caribbean Premier League and IPL. Throughout his ODI career, he amassed over 2,000 runs and claimed 69 wickets in 136 matches.
