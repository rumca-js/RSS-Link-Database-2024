[1144366] Oman interested in using Iran’s Imam Khomeini Port trade capacities

https://www.tehrantimes.com/rss
2024-09-14 10:32:23+00:00
TEHRAN – A business delegation from Oman has visited Iran’s Imam Khomeini Port in southwestern Khuzestan province during which they expressed interest in using the commercial capacities of the port for expanding economic ties with Iran.
