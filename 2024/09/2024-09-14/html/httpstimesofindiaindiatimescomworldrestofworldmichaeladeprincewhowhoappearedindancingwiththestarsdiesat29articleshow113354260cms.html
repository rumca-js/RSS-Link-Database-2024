[1145290] Michaela DePrince, who appeared in 'Dancing with the Stars', dies at 29

https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/rssfeedstopstories.cms
2024-09-14 16:16:38.471979+00:00
Michaela Mabinty DePrince, a Sierra Leonean ballerina who appeared in "Dancing with the Stars" and Beyonce's "Lemonade," died unexpectedly at age 29. Her family confirmed the news on social media, expressing deep sorrow. DePrince's inspiring journey from war orphan to acclaimed dancer touched many lives globally.
