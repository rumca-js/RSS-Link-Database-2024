India's Shreyas Iyer faced criticism from former Pakistan cricketer Basit Ali after being dismissed for a seven-ball duck in the Duleep Trophy. Ali suggested that Iyer missed an opportunity to make a significant contribution and questioned his hunger for Test cricket. Iyer has been excluded from the Indian Test squad due to consistent poor performances.
