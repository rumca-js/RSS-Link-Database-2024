Dead Rising has garnered somewhat of a cult following since it first dropped in 2006, but despite its quiet success there have always been several complaints which, gracefully, the developers have addressed in this Deluxe Remaster coming on September 19th. 

Graphical overhauls are expected, crafted via the RE Engine – but quality of life improvements, boosts to gameplay and user experience, will elevate Dead Rising Deluxe Edition to one of the foremost zombie survival horrors, if not one of the biggest games, come the year’s end.
