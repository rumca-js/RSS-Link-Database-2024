[1144017] 'Madhabi Buch investing in Chinese firms': Cong ups ante against Sebi chief

https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/rssfeedstopstories.cms
2024-09-14 10:51:18.136972+00:00
Congress has accused Sebi chief Madhabi Puri-Buch of making high-value investments in Chinese firms during geopolitical tensions and trading in listed securities with unpublished price-sensitive information. Buch and her husband issued a statement denying any conflict of interest involving Agora Advisory Pvt, Mahindra Group, Dr Reddy's, Pidilite, among others.
