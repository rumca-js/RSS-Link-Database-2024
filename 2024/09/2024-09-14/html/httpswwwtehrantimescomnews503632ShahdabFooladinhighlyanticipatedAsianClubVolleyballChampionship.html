TEHRAN - The 2024 Asian Men’s Club Volleyball Championship is culminating in an all-Iranian showdown as Shahdab Yazd and Foolad Sirjan prepare to battle for the continental title on Sunday. This highly anticipated final promises an intense match between two of Iran’s most formidable volleyball clubs.
