[1144016] FIR filed over fireworks outside Arvind Kejriwal's residence

https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/rssfeedstopstories.cms
2024-09-14 10:51:15.925315+00:00
Delhi Police have lodged an FIR over firecrackers being set off outside Chief Minister Arvind Kejriwal's residence after his release from jail. This follows a government ban on firecrackers to combat winter air pollution. The Supreme Court granted bail to Kejriwal in a corruption case related to the Delhi excise policy, leading to his release.
