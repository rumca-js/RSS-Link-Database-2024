Amid the major controversy over rumors that Haitians are eating cats and dogs in Ohio's Springfield, a new video posted by Christopher F Rufo, claimed that African migrants last year were filmed grilling cats in Dayton. And the claim found its backing from GOP's vice presidential candidate JD Vance who condemned how the story "debunked" by media turned out to have merit. "Kamala Harris and her media apparatchiks should be ashamed of themselves," Vance said sharing Christopher's post.
