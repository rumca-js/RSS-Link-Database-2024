The India vs Pakistan hockey rivalry continues to captivate fans despite Pakistan's recent struggles. India have dominated their last 16 encounters with Pakistan, winning 14 times, while two matches ended as draws. The historic clashes include three Olympic finals and the 1975 World Cup final. The two teams take each other on once again in the Asian Champions Trophy 2024.
