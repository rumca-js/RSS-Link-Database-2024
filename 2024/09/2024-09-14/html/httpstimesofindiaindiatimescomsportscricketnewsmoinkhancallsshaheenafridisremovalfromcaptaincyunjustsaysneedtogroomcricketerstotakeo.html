[1143175] Moin Khan calls Afridi's removal from Pakistan captaincy 'unjust'

https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/rssfeedstopstories.cms
2024-09-14 05:09:49.192295+00:00
Former Pakistan captain Moin Khan has criticized the decision to remove Shaheen Shah Afridi from white-ball captaincy, highlighting his leadership skills and popularity among players. He emphasized that frequent changes in captaincy negatively impact player performance and stressed the need for a stable, long-term approach in grooming future leaders for Pakistan cricket.
