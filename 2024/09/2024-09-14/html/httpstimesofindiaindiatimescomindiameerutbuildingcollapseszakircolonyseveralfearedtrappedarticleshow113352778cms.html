[1145036] Building collapses in Meerut’s Zakir Colony, several feared trapped

https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/rssfeedstopstories.cms
2024-09-14 15:11:15.364949+00:00
A building collapse in Zakir Colony of Meerut has left 8-10 people trapped. Rescue efforts are ongoing with police and fire officials at the site. The Army, NDRF, and SDRF have been alerted for additional support, according to Selva Kumari, Commissioner of Meerut Division.
