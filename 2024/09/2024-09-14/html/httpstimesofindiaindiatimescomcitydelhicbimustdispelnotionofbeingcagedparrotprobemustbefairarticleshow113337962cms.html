[1143068] ‘Caged parrot’ rap haunts CBI after 11 years

https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/rssfeedstopstories.cms
2024-09-14 04:06:03.636758+00:00
The Supreme Court has raised concerns over the CBI's arrest of Delhi CM Arvind Kejriwal, questioning its motives and timing. Justice Ujjal Bhuyan highlighted the need for fair, transparent investigations, likening the CBI to a 'caged parrot' under influence. He stressed that arrests should be used sparingly and fairly.
