Grand Theft Auto is a highly successful franchise from Rockstar, and it’s not hard to understand why it has been so popular with gamers. It’s rebellious, action-packed, and features some of the most memorable open worlds in the gaming industry. With that said, the franchise is not without its flaws. 

Some of these flaws are found in GTA 4 which features annoying car handling and repetitive missions. Furthermore, there’s not much to do once players are done with the main story. Other problems emerge with GTA Online which features intrusive microtransactions and is constantly plagued by player griefing. Perhaps one of the biggest problems is that the wait time between titles has stretched considerably, leaving fans without a new GTA title for years.
