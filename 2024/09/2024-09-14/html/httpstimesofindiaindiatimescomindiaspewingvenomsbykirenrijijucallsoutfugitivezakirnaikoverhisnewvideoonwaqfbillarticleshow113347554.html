Union minister Kiren Rijiju denounced Islamic preacher Zakir Naik for disseminating false claims about the Waqf bill. Rijiju emphasized the need for unity against such divisive figures. The Waqf Amendment Bill, which aims to reform the management of Waqf properties, is currently being reviewed by a Joint Parliamentary Committee for broader consultation.
