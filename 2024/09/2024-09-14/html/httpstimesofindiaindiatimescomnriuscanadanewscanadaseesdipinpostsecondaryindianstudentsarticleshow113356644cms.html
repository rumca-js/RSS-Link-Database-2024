[1146315] Canada sees dip in post-secondary Indian students

https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/rssfeedstopstories.cms
2024-09-14 21:40:43.356098+00:00
Canada approved 55,500 post-secondary study permits for Indian students from January to June 2024, accounting for 49% of international students. Despite an impressive approval rate of 85%, there's a noticeable decline in applications. Global interest in studying in Canada has decreased by nearly 20%, with other countries gaining traction.
