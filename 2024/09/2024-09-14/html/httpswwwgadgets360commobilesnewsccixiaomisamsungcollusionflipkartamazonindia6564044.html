[1144095] CCI Accuses Samsung, Xiaomi of Colluding With Amazon and Flipkart

https://feeds.feedburner.com/gadgets360-latest
2024-09-14 10:55:50.402575+00:00
Samsung, Xiaomi and other smartphone manufacturers have been accused of colluding with Amazon and Walmart's Flipkart for exclusive product launches on their Indian e-commerce platforms, violating antitrust laws, as per regulatory reports from the Competition Commission of India. The involvement of smartphone manufacturers such as Samsung and Xiaomi in the lawsuit could escalate their legal and regulatory challenges in the country.
