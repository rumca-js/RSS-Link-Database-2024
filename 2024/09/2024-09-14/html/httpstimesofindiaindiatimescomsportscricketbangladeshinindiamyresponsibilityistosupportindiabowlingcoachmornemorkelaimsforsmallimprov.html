Morne Morkel, newly appointed as India's bowling coach, intends to make minor adjustments rather than overhauling the efficient cricket system. He aims to build trust with players and support senior members like Rohit Sharma and Virat Kohli. Morkel's focus is on helping players manage the pressures of representing India by leveraging his extensive experience.
