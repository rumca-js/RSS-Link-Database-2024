[1145286] Prashant Kishor vows to end Bihar liquor ban within 60 mins of taking office

https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/rssfeedstopstories.cms
2024-09-14 16:16:27.354307+00:00
Prashant Kishor promised to end Bihar’s liquor ban if elected, criticizing Nitish Kumar and Tejashwi Yadav for their leadership. He questioned Tejashwi’s educational qualifications and capability. Kishor’s Jan Suraj Abhiyan will launch a political party on October 2, aiming to contest all 243 seats in the upcoming Bihar assembly elections.
