[1144542] Govt extends deadline for updation of Aadhaar card details

https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/rssfeedstopstories.cms
2024-09-14 13:01:45.606054+00:00
The Unique Identification Authority of India (UIDAI) has extended the deadline for free online updates to Aadhaar card details until December 14, 2024. This extension enables all Aadhaar card holders to update crucial information such as address, name, mobile number and even photographs on the myAadhaar portal at no cost.
