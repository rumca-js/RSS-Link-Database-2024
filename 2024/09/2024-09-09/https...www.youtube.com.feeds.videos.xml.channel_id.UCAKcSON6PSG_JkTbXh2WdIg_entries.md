Bay Area four-piece indie-rock band Shannon and the Clams visited The Current for a studio session hosted by Jessica Paxton. After the performance, frontperson Shannon Shaw (and her dog, Spanky Joe), hung out for a heartfelt conversation with Jessica about the inspirations behind the album, "The Moon Is In The Wrong Place."
