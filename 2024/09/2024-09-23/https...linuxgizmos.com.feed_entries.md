The MS-CF16 is a compact Pico-ITX single-board computer designed for fanless, low-power, high-performance applications in harsh environments. Powered by Intel Alder Lake-N or Amston Lake Series SoCs, the board features a 2.5GbE LAN port, a GbE LAN port, and SATA 3.0 for storage.
