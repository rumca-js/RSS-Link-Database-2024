## How to Build Schluter Shower Curb - #shorts #homerepairtutor
 - [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0JwaMb-dTFI](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0JwaMb-dTFI)

 - date published: 2024-09-23T11:00:00+00:00

I used solid 2-inch KERDI-BOARD then added two 1/2-inch pieces. Thin-set bonds them to the subfloor, walls, pan, and each other. The curb is level left to right but pitched slightly toward the shower. This promotes water drainage. Ultimately I waterproofed everything using KERDI-BAND along with KERDI-KERECK prefab corners
