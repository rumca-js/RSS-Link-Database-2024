## Discovery and invention

Isaac Newton didn&#8217;t invent gravity. It was there all along. He simply named and explained it. The same is true for planets, continents and obscure species. They&#8217;re discovered, not invented. Michelangelo talked about removing all the parts of the marble that weren&#8217;t the statue on his way to creating great art. Discovery is like that.
