Until Dawn is a title that's earned some respect among horror fans. It's one of the best games of the PS4 era with its presentation, writing, and overall breadth of outcomes while also helping establish Supermassive Games as a major developer.

Though it's been relegated to the previous generation with no hope of an upgrade or remaster, Sony is giving it the remake treatment. Launching on October 4th for PS5 and PC, Ballistic Moon's Until Dawn rebuilds the title from the ground up on Unreal Engine 5, retaining the core story while adding many new features.

The visuals have received multiple improvements, like injury mask updates for more realistic cuts and bruises and improved textures and animations. A new third-person camera offers a fresh perspective on the game's action, while the Prologue has been reworked. Check out everything you should know about Until Dawn's remake before it launches.
