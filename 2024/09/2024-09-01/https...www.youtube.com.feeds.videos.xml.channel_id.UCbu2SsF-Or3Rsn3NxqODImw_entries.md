Elden Ring is filled to the brim with rich and detailed lore created by the minds of Hidetaka Miyazaki, George RR Martin, and the team at FromSoftware. However, like most of FromSoftware’s recent games, the story and lore are puzzles players need to piece together to fully understand. In this video, DaveControl pieces together the lore, to the best of his understanding. It’s well worth watching other YouTubers to see where you land, and find where your personal interpretation lies.
