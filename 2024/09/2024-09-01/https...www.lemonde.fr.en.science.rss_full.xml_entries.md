## In the US, many cancers are becoming increasingly common in young people
 - [https://www.lemonde.fr/en/united-states/article/2024/09/01/in-the-us-many-cancers-are-becoming-increasingly-common-in-young-people_6724212_133.html](https://www.lemonde.fr/en/united-states/article/2024/09/01/in-the-us-many-cancers-are-becoming-increasingly-common-in-young-people_6724212_133.html)
 - RSS feed: https://www.lemonde.fr/en/science/rss_full.xml
 - date published: 2024-09-01T01:07:27+00:00

A major American study has determined that generations born in the 1980s and 1990s are more likely to develop cancer than their elders. However, these findings are not solely due to an aging population, but sometimes due to earlier diagnoses. Fortunately, the incidence of tobacco-related tumors is decreasing, unlike that of cancers linked to pollution and diet.
