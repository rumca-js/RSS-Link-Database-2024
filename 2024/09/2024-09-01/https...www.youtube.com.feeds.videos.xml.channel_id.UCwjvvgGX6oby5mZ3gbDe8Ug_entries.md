Recently, there have been many reports of chaos in Zhongnanhai. Since late July, rumors about Chinese President Xi Jinping have swirled. Including he has had a stroke, that he's too sick to make decisions, that he's losing power due to a military coup, and that he has even died. These rumors have been prevalent on overseas Chinese websites. These are partly due to the perception that Xi Jinping almost disappeared between July and mid-August of this year. There were few videos of him attending events in the Chinese media. But in late August, he began to expose many retaliatory incidents.
