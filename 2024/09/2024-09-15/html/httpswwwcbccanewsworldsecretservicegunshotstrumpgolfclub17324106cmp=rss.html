[1149980] Trump safe after gunshots reported near his Florida golf club, Secret Service says

https://www.cbc.ca/webfeed/rss/rss-topstories
2024-09-15 15:17:01+00:00
Donald Trump is safe after gunshots were reported in his vicinity Sunday afternoon in Florida, his campaign and the Secret Service said. It was not immediately clear whether the reported shots were targeted at the Republican presidential nominee.
