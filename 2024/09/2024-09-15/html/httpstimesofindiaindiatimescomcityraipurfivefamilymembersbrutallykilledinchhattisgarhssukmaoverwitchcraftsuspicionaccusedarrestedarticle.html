[1148443] Five of a family killed in Chhattisgarh over witchcraft suspicion

https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/rssfeedstopstories.cms
2024-09-15 12:34:59.008970+00:00
In Sukma district, Chhattisgarh, five family members were killed over suspicions of witchcraft. The accused have surrendered and been arrested. Villagers believed the victims caused health issues and personal losses. Police are investigating the case from all angles and dismissed any Maoist involvement.
