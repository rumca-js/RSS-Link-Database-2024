Delhi CM Arvind Kejriwal announced his resignation within two days, leading social activist Anna Hazare to express disappointment. Hazare criticized Kejriwal for ignoring his advice to avoid politics and focus on social service. Previously, Hazare condemned Kejriwal's actions related to the Delhi excise case, attributing his arrest to his own deeds.
