Garrick; Arcola; Lyric Hammersmith, London 
Dating disasters fuel a joyful new musical from the creators of Six; three old band members reunite in Barney Norris’s low-key elegy to lost youth. Plus, a hit-and-miss revival of Timberlake Wertenbaker’s penal colony classic
“Good food. Good coffee. Great conversation.” Ah, yes, those unique interests that every man on every dating app ever somehow thinks will make him stand out in the Meet Market – as it’s winningly described in a punchy number in the new “big fancy musical” Why Am I So Single? at the Garrick.
The song occurs in the first act of this sweet, silly, deceptively smart and surprisingly moving show about two late-millennial besties, both scarred by the modern dating experience.
