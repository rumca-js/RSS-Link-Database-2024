https://www.theguardian.com/international/rss
2024-09-15 17:02:02+00:00
  • Teenager sets side on way to 4-1 victory at Girona
  • Barça have won all five league games this season
Barcelona’s Lamine Yamal struck twice in the first half to help deliver a commanding 4-1 win at Girona on Sunday that extended their perfect start to La Liga’s season with a fifth consecutive win.
Yamal gave Barça a two-goal lead with strikes in the 30th and 37th minutes while Dani Olmo extended their advantage right after the break with a first-time effort from close range.
