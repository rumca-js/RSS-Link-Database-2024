[1148687] Kinetic Green targets Rs 10,000 cr revenue with new platforms, B2C focus

https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/rssfeedstopstories.cms
2024-09-15 13:40:43.415530+00:00
Kinetic Green is shifting from its B2B model to a broader B2C approach with an ambitious target of Rs 10,000 crore revenue by 2030. The company focuses on two- and three-wheeler electric vehicles. Founder Sulajja Firodia Motwani highlights new models, platform development, scalability, efficiency, and minimal reliance on subsidies as key strategies.
