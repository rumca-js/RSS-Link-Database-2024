[1147028] Comoros president's attacker found dead in prison cell, investigation underway

https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/rssfeedstopstories.cms
2024-09-15 03:13:20.708756+00:00
A soldier who attacked Comoros President Azali Assoumani with a knife during a funeral was found dead in his prison cell under unclear circumstances. Authorities are investigating both the motive behind the attack and the cause of his death. The president sustained minor injuries but is out of danger.
