[1149240] Kejriwal says he will resign in 48 hrs: Does this mean early polls in Delhi?

https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/rssfeedstopstories.cms
2024-09-15 15:30:17+00:00
Delhi Chief Minister Arvind Kejriwal announced he will resign from his post within two days, potentially leading to early assembly elections. He stated he would only return if voters affirm his honesty. This decision has stirred political discourse and speculation regarding AAP's interim leadership and electoral strategy. Kejriwal further indicated that his deputy Manish Sisodia will also return to office only after the people give a verdict in his favour. The BJP has termed the announcement a "PR stunt".
