[1147457] Watch: Siddaramaiah's security breached as man runs towards stage

https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/rssfeedstopstories.cms
2024-09-15 08:13:21.601400+00:00
At a Democracy Day event in Bengaluru, an unidentified man attempted to reach Karnataka CM Siddaramaiah on stage but was intercepted by security personnel. The individual, identified as Mahadev Nayak, intended to garland the CM with a shawl. Siddaramaiah remained unharmed while Nayak was detained for violating security protocols.
