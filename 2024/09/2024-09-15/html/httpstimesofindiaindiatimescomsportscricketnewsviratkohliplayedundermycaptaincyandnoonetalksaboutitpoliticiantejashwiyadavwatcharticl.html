[1147230] 'Virat Kohli played under my captaincy': Tejashwi Yadav

https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/rssfeedstopstories.cms
2024-09-15 05:25:05.923159+00:00
Tejashwi Yadav, son of former Bihar Prime Minister Lalu Prasad Yadav, shared his cricketing journey on social media. He claimed to have captained Virat Kohli in domestic cricket and expressed disappointment over the lack of recognition for his achievements. Tejashwi played for Jharkhand and was part of Delhi Daredevils in the IPL from 2008 to 2012.
