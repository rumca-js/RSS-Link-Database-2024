A Texas jury is determining whether Trump supporters intimidated Wendy Davis and others on a Biden-Harris campaign bus during the 2020 election. The plaintiffs claim that the convoy's actions violated state and federal laws, including the Ku Klux Klan Act. Defendants argue that their actions were merely protected political speech.
