[1148625] Piastri wins superb Azerbaijan F1 GP as Norris eats into Verstappen’s title lead

https://www.theguardian.com/rss
2024-09-15 13:15:09+00:00
  • McLaren driver victorious after duel with Charles Leclerc
  • Sergio Pérez and Carlos Sainz eliminated by late crash
Oscar Piastri won the Azerbaijan Grand Prix with an exceptional drive for McLaren after an enormously tense battle to the flag with Ferrari’s Charles Leclerc in Baku. Lando Norris made a superb and unlikely comeback drive to take fourth having started in 15th to keep his title hopes alive, finishing in front of title rival Max Verstappen who was fifth.
George Russell was fourth for Mercedes after a late crash between Red Bull’s Sergio Pérez and Ferrari’s Carlos Sainz eliminated both drivers. Lewis Hamilton was in ninth having started at the back of the grid with an engine penalty.
