[1148446] Netflix denies filmmaker Soham Shah’s Squid Games plagiarism allegations

https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/rssfeedstopstories.cms
2024-09-15 12:35:05.839805+00:00
Netflix refutes claims from filmmaker Soham Shah that the Korean series 'Squid Games' plagiarised his film 'Luck'. Shah alleges both works involve deadly games for a jackpot. Netflix maintains that 'Squid Game' is an original creation by Hwang Dong-hyuk, who began working on it in 2009.
