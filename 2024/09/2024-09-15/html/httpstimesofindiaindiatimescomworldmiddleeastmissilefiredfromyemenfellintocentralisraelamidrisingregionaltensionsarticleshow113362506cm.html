[1147475] Missile fired from Yemen fell into Central Israel amid rising regional tensions

https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/rssfeedstopstories.cms
2024-09-15 08:15:10.649340+00:00
The Israeli military reported that a surface-to-surface missile fired from Yemen crossed into central Israel and landed in an open area, causing no injuries. This incident is part of ongoing attacks by Yemen's Huthi rebels in solidarity with Palestinians amid the Israel-Hamas conflict. Hostilities have intensified across the region since Hamas's October 7 attack on Israel.
