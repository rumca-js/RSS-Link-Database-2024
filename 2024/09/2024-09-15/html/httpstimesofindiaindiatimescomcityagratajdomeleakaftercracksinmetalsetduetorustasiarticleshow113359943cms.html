[1147110] Taj Mahal's dome leak due to cracks in metal set due to rust: ASI

https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/rssfeedstopstories.cms
2024-09-15 04:19:23.766769+00:00
The Taj Mahal in Agra is experiencing water seepage and dampness within its main tombs following prolonged rainfall. Experts from the Archaeological Survey of India inspected the site, finding rust and potential cracks in the dome. Repair work will be undertaken to prevent future leakage and address moisture issues inside the tombs.
