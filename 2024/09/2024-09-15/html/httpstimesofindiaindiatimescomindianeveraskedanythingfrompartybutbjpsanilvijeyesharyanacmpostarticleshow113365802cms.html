[1148182] 'Never asked anything from party but ...': BJP's Anil Vij eyes Haryana CM post

https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/rssfeedstopstories.cms
2024-09-15 11:29:28.013022+00:00
Senior BJP leader Anil Vij announced he would stake a claim for the chief minister's post in Haryana, citing public demand and his seniority. This comes despite Nayab Singh Saini being named as the party's candidate. "I am the party's senior-most MLA and have won six elections and am contesting my seventh one. I have never sought anything from my party so far," he said.
