Prince William extended birthday wishes to his brother Harry on his 40th birthday despite their strained relationship. The brothers, who were once close, have not communicated for two years. Harry expressed excitement about turning 40 and highlighted the joys of fatherhood. Reconciliation between them is not expected soon.
