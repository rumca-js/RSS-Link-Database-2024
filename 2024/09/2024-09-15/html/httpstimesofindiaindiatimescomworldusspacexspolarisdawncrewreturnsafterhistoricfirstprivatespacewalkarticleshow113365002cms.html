The Polaris Dawn crew completed a five-day mission aboard SpaceX's Dragon capsule, featuring the first commercial spacewalk led by Jared Isaacman. The team reached an altitude of 875 miles and conducted over 40 experiments. This mission is considered a significant advancement for private space exploration.
