[1150780] SI, 2 other cops suspended for 'booze party' at thana in Bihar

https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/rssfeedstopstories.cms
2024-09-15 23:52:59.378135+00:00
A sub-inspector and two chowkidars in Bihar's Kaimur district were suspended after being caught consuming alcohol on duty at Sonhan police station. A breath analyser confirmed their intoxication, leading to court proceedings and departmental actions. Urine and blood samples of the trio have been sent for laboratory testing.
