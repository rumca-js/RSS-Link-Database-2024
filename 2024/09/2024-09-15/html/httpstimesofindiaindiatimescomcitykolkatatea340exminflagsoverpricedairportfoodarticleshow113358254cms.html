[1147770] 'Its just hot water': Tea for Rs 340 at airport shocks Chidambaram, travelers

https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/rssfeedstopstories.cms
2024-09-15 04:21:14+00:00
Former Union finance minister P Chidambaram flagged overpriced food and beverages at Kolkata airport, mentioning a Rs 340 charge for tea. The airport management assured an investigation into the matter. The Coffee Bean and Tea Leaf justified their pricing based on brand standards. Flyers have consistently reported high prices at the airport.
