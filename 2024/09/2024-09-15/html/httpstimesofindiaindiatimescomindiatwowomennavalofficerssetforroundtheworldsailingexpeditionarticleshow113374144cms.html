[1150231] Two women naval officers set for round-the-world sailing expedition

https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/rssfeedstopstories.cms
2024-09-15 20:38:41.958506+00:00
Lieutenant Commanders Roopa A and Dilna K are set to embark on a global circumnavigation aboard the INSV Tarini after three years of intense training. The expedition, 'Navika Sagar Parikrama-II', will challenge their sailing skills and endurance. They have already completed multiple trans-oceanic voyages as part of their preparation.
