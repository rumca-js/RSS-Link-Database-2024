[1149244] Pakistan clarifies ban on Twitter as the country probes ex-PM Imran Khan’s post

https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/rssfeedstopstories.cms
2024-09-15 15:50:38.932842+00:00
The Pakistan Telecommunication Authority notified the Sindh High Court that services of Elon Musk-owned platform X remain suspended in Pakistan due to a miscommunication. Separately, the Federal Investigation Agency completed an initial probe into former PM Imran Khan’s controversial posts on X, which raised national safety concerns and involved questioning at Adiala Jail.
