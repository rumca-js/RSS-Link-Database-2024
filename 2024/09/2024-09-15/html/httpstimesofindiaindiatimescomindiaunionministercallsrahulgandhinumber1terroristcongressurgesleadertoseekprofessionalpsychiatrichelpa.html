Punjab opposition leader Partap Singh Bajwa criticised Union minister Ravneet Singh Bittu for his derogatory remarks against Congress MP Rahul Gandhi. Bajwa labeled Bittu's comments as irresponsible and suggested he seek mental health support. Bittu accused Gandhi of being a terrorist and sympathizing with separatists, leading to significant controversy.
