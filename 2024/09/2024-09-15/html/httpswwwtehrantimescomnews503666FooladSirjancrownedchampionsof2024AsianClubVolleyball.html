[1149324] Foolad Sirjan crowned champions of 2024 Asian Club Volleyball Championship

https://www.tehrantimes.com/rss
2024-09-15 15:31:06+00:00
TEHRAN – Foolad Sirjan Iranian defeated Shahdab Yazd in straight sets (25-20, 25-18, 25-23) in an all-Iranian final of the 2024 Asian Men’s Club Volleyball Championship.
