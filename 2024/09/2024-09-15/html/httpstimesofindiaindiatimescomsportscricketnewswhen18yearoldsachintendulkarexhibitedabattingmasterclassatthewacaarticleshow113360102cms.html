[1146899] When Sachin Tendulkar exhibited a batting masterclass at the WACA

Sachin Tendulkar's illustrious career in Australia includes standout performances against top bowlers. His innings at the WACA Ground in Perth as an 18-year-old is particularly memorable. Despite India's defeat, Tendulkar's century on a challenging pitch remains one of the greatest knocks by an overseas batsman in Australia, earning him immense respect. Tendulkar's performances in Australia solidified his place as one of cricket's all-time greats.
