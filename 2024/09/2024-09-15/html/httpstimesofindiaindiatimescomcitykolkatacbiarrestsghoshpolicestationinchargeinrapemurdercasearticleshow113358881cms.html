[1146898] CBI arrests Ghosh, police station in-charge in rape-murder case

https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/rssfeedstopstories.cms
2024-09-15 02:08:00.495250+00:00
CBI arrested former RG Kar Medical College principal Sandip Ghosh and Tala police station OC Abhijit Mandal for destroying evidence related to the rape-murder of a 31-year-old doctor. This follows the earlier arrest of civic volunteer Sanjay Roy. The case raises concerns about delays in filing FIR and conducting postmortem procedures.
