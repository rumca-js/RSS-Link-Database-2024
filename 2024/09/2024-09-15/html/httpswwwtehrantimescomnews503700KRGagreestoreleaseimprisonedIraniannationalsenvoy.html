[1149546] KRG agrees to release imprisoned Iranian nationals: envoy

https://www.tehrantimes.com/rss
2024-09-15 16:40:11+00:00
TEHRAN – Iran's ambassador to Baghdad, Mohammad Kazem Al-e Sadeq has revealed that Kurdistan Regional Government officials have agreed to cooperate with Tehran on the release or extradition of Iranian nationals currently held in Erbil and Sulaymaniyah.
