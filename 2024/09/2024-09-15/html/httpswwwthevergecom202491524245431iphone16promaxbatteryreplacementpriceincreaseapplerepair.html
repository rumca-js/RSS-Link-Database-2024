[1149789] Apple now charges more to swap your iPhone 16 Pro battery

https://www.theverge.com/rss/index.xml
2024-09-15 14:26:50+00:00


Apple has hiked the price of its battery repair service for the iPhone 16 Pro and Pro Max. Now, it will cost $119 to get your battery replaced in either phone — a $20 jump over its older fee. The price remains the same for the standard and “Plus” models, as do those for last year’s phones, as MacRumors notes.
