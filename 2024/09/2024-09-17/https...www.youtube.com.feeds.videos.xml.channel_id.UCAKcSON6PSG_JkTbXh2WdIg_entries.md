Americana singer-songwriter Cassandra Lewis put on a stunning performance at Surly Brewing Co. in Minneapolis on Thursday, July 11, 2024, accompanied on guitar and backing vocals by Trevor Bahnson. Before the main performance, Lewis and Bahnson performed this take of "More Like Mama" on the Surly Brewing grounds.

The show, part of The Current’s Happy Hours series, came a day before the official release of her major-label debut, "Lost In a Dream." The album was recorded in Savannah, Georgia, with producer Dave Cobb (Brandi Carlile, Jason Isbell, Sturgill Simpson). "More Like Mama" appears on "Lost In a Dream," available on @ElektraEntertainment.
