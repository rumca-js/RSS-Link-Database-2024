## After the invisible man, the (partially) transparent mouse
 - [https://www.lemonde.fr/en/science/article/2024/09/09/after-the-invisible-man-the-partially-transparent-mouse_6725304_10.html](https://www.lemonde.fr/en/science/article/2024/09/09/after-the-invisible-man-the-partially-transparent-mouse_6725304_10.html)
 - RSS feed: https://www.lemonde.fr/en/science/rss_full.xml
 - date published: 2024-09-08T22:30:08+00:00

On September 5, a research team from the United States revealed in the journal Science that it had achieved a groundbreaking advance in biomedical imaging. By applying a yellow dye known as tartrazine directly to the skin of rodents, the team succeeded in visualizing internal structures with unprecedented clarity.
