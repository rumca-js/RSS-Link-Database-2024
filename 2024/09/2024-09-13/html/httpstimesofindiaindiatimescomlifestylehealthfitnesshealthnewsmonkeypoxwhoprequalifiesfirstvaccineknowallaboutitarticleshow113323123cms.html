[1138348] Monkeypox: WHO prequalifies first vaccine; know all about it

https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/rssfeedstopstories.cms
2024-09-13 11:31:49.967154+00:00
The WHO has added the MVA-BN vaccine by Bavarian Nordic A/S to its list of prequalified vaccines against monkeypox, aiming to enhance access in high-need regions. The vaccine is effective at 76% with one dose and over 80% with two doses. Monkeypox was declared a global emergency in August 2024 amid widespread outbreaks.
