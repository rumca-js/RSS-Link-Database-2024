[1139348] David Beckham joins mourners at Sven-Göran Eriksson’s funeral in Sweden

https://www.theguardian.com/world/rss
2024-09-13 14:46:55+00:00
Tributes paid to ‘genuinely kind’ former England and Lazio football manager in his home town of Torsby
  • Sven-Göran Eriksson obituary
David Beckham joined mourners at the funeral of former England manager Sven-Göran Eriksson in his home town of Torsby in Sweden to hear tributes to a “genuinely kind” man who “loved good food and drink and travelling first class”.
Addressing full pews in the Fryksande church, and hundreds more watching outside on a big screen, priest and close friend, Ingela Alvskog, spoke of a final months that had been “full of life” despite Eriksson’s diagnosis with terminal cancer of the pancreas.
