[1138057] Electricity generation from renewable sources rises 23%

https://www.tehrantimes.com/rss
2024-09-13 10:18:16+00:00
TEHRAN - Iranian renewable power plants generated 264 million kilowatt-hours (Kwh) of electricity in the previous Iranian calendar month of Mordad (July 22-August 22), registering a 23-percent growth compared to the same month in the previous year.
