[1142607] Health Canada Recalls Jamp Brand Acetaminophen, Other Medications

https://feed.theepochtimes.com/world/feed
2024-09-13 23:46:36+00:00
Health Canada has issued a recall for several medications, including acetaminophen, distributed by Canadian pharmaceutical firm Jamp Pharma. Jamp’s 325 milligram and 500 milligram tablets of acetaminophen are being recalled because they may contain inaccurate dosages of the active ingredient, Health Canada said.
