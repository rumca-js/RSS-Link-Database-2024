[1140066] 'Vow to fight anti-national forces': Kejriwal after walking out of jail

https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/rssfeedstopstories.cms
2024-09-13 16:07:13.135519+00:00
Delhi CM Arvind Kejriwal walked out of Tihar jail after four months, granted bail by the Supreme Court in the excise policy case. He thanked supporters and pledged to fight divisive forces. Party members hailed his release as a triumph for AAP and democracy, celebrating with fireworks.
