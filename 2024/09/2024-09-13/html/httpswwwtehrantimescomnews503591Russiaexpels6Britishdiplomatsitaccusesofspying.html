[1140581] Russia expels 6 British diplomats it accuses of spying

https://www.tehrantimes.com/rss
2024-09-13 16:41:22+00:00
Russia on Friday accused six British diplomats of spying and said it decided to expel them. The UK said the “completely baseless” move came weeks ago and was linked to its action in May to revoke the credentials of an attaché at the Russian Embassy and limit Moscow’s diplomatic activities in London, AP reported.
