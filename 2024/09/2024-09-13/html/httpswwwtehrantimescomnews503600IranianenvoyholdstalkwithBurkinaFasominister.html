[1140591]  Iranian envoy holds talk with Burkina Faso minister

https://www.tehrantimes.com/rss
2024-09-13 16:34:32+00:00
TEHRAN - Mojtaba Faghihi, Iran’s ambassador to Burkina Faso, recently engaged in discussions with Abubakar Nakanabo, the Minister of Economy and Finance of Burkina Faso, focusing on enhancing bilateral economic, technical, and commercial collaboration between their nations.
