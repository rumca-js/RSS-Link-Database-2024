John Doe and Exene Cervenka of the legendary Los Angeles rock band X visited The Current to perform three discography-spanning songs in The Current studio. After the performance, Doe and Cervenka spent some time conversing with host Jessica Paxton, reflecting on their origins in Los Angeles in the crucible of the emerging punk scene.
