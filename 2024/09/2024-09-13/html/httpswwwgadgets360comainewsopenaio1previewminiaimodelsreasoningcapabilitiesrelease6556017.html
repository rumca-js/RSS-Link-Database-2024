[1138033] OpenAI o1 Series AI Models With Advanced Reasoning Capabilities Released

https://feeds.feedburner.com/gadgets360-latest
2024-09-13 10:34:37.388977+00:00
OpenAI released the new o1 series artificial intelligence (AI) models on Thursday. The AI firm is calling these reasoning models for their advanced capabilities in solving mathematical and complex reasoning-based problems. There are two models — o1 which is available in preview, and o1-mini — in this iteration. The company said these AI models were trained to spend time thinking before they respond, similar to humans.
