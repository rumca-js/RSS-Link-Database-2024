[1139412] Infinix Xpad With 11-Inch Full-HD+ Display, LTE Support Launched in India: Price, Specifications

https://feeds.feedburner.com/gadgets360-latest
2024-09-13 15:21:05.824453+00:00
Infinix Xpad was launched in India on Friday. It is the first tablet offering from the company. The tablet sports an 11-inch full-HD+ screen, an 8-megapixel main camera and a quad speaker unit. It supports Wi-Fi as well as 4G LTE connectivity and ships with Android 14-based XOS 14. The tablet will be available for purchase in the country later this month.
