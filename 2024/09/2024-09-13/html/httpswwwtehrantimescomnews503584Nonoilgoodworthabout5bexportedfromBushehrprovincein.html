[1139451] Non-oil good worth about $5b exported from Bushehr province in 5 months

https://www.tehrantimes.com/rss
2024-09-13 13:09:56+00:00
TEHRAN- Non-oil commodities valued at about $5 billion were exported from Bushehr province, in the southwest of Iran, during the first five months of the current Iranian calendar year (March 20-Auhust 21), according to a provincial official.
