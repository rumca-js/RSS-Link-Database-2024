[1137390] NRL star Josh Addo-Carr’s secondary drug test returns positive result for cocaine

https://www.theguardian.com/international/rss
2024-09-13 06:38:27+00:00
  • Oral sample from roadside test last week comes back positive
  • Bulldogs winger stood himself down after initial positive test
Canterbury winger Josh Addo-Carr has returned a second positive result to a drugs test. The Bulldogs star stood himself down from NRL finals duty after an initial drug wipe returned a positive reading during a roadside test last week, while a second sample was taken away by police for analysis.
The results of that analysis were revealed a week later on Friday – 48 hours out from the Bulldogs’ highly-anticipated NRL finals clash with Manly.
