[1141231] Driver charged with killing NHL’s Johnny Gaudreau and brother had .087% BAC

https://www.theguardian.com/international/rss
2024-09-13 17:29:59+00:00
  • NHL star and brother were killed on 29 August
  • Sean M Higgins faces up to 20 years in prison 
The driver charged with killing NHL hockey player Johnny Gaudreau and his brother Matthew as they bicycled on a rural road had a blood-alcohol level of .087, above the .08 legal limit in New Jersey, a prosecutor said Friday.
Gaudreau, 31, and brother Matthew, 29, were killed near their childhood home in South Jersey on 29 August, the evening before they were set to serve as groomsmen at their sister Katie’s wedding.
