[1140175] Elgato launches a giant Stream Deck for professionals

https://www.pcworld.com/feed
2024-09-13 15:28:24+00:00




Elgato has now launched its largest Stream Deck yet, the Elgato Stream Deck Studio. The live streaming tool features a whopping 32 programmable LCD buttons in two rows as well as a pair of rotary dials. This compares to Elgato’s original Stream Deck, which has 15 buttons. The Deck Studio, however, is intended specifically for professional users and traditional studio broadcasters. 


The Elgato Stream Deck Studio measures almost half a meter in length and connects with a single USB-C input on the front. There are also two more USB-C ports on the back, one of which offers connection to a PC and another can be used to power the device instead of the Stream Deck Studio’s 25W Ethernet connection. It’s also possible to connect several Elgato Stream Deck Studios to each other.
