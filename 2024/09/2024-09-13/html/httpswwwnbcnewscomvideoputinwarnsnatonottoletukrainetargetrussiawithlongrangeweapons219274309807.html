[1137939] Putin warns the West not to allow Ukraine to target Russia with long-range weapons

https://feeds.nbcnews.com/nbcnews/public/world
2024-09-13 09:03:37+00:00
Russian President Vladimir Putin said on Thursday that the West would be directly fighting with Russia if it allowed Ukraine to strike Russian territory with Western-made long-range missiles, a move he said would alter the nature and scope of the conflict.
