[1137653] Haryana: It’s Bhupinder Singh Hooda way or the highway in Congress

https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/rssfeedstopstories.cms
2024-09-13 09:19:41+00:00
Former Haryana CM Bhupinder Singh Hooda outmaneuvered other state leaders by securing 72 tickets for his loyalists in the upcoming assembly elections. Rival Kumari Selja managed only nine. Both Congress and BJP have fielded many candidates from political families, underscoring the prevalence of family politics in these elections.
