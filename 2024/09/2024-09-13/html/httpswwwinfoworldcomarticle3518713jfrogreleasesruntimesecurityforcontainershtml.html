[1136308] JFrog Platform adds runtime security for containers

https://www.infoworld.com/feed/
2024-09-13 00:20:20+00:00










With the introduction of JFrog Runtime, JFrog has added a component to its software supply chain security and devsecops platform to help users secure container-based applications in production.


Introduced September 10, JFrog Runtime integrates with JFrog Artifactory to allow devops and security teams to easily identify the source and owner of a vulnerable package and determine the fastest and most efficient way to mitigate risks, JFrog said.
