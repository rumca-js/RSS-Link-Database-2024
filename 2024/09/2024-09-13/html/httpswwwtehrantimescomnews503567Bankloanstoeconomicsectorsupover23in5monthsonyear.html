[1138481] Bank loans to economic sectors up over 23% in 5 months on year

https://www.tehrantimes.com/rss
2024-09-13 10:22:57+00:00
TEHRAN - The Iranian banking system has paid 25.29 quadrillion rials (about $50.58 billion) of facilities to various economic sectors in the first five months of the current Iranian calendar year (March 20 -August 21), registering a 23.9 percent rise from the figure in the same period of the previous year, the Central Bank of Iran (CBI) announced.
