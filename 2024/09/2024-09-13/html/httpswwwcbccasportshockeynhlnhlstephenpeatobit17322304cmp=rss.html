[1138579] Former NHL enforcer Stephen Peat dies 2 weeks after being struck by car in Langley, B.C.

https://www.cbc.ca/webfeed/rss/rss-topstories
2024-09-13 07:40:14+00:00
Stephen Peat, the former Washington Capitals enforcer who fought concussion issues and experienced homelessness at times after leaving hockey, has died from injuries sustained late last month when he was struck by a car while crossing a street in Langley, B.C. He was 44.
