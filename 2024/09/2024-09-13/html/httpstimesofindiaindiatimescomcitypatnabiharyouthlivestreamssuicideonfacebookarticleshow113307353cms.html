[1137017] Man from Patna livestreams his suicide, jumps into river Ganga

https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/rssfeedstopstories.cms
2024-09-13 06:09:05.824663+00:00
Amit Kumar Yadav, a 28-year-old man from Patna, ended his life by jumping into the Ganga while livestreaming the incident. He held his father, brother, and girlfriend responsible for his actions. His body was found after a 10-hour search by SDRF. Initial findings suggest he was mentally unstable and depressed.
