[1137018] TMC’s Mahua Moitra files complaint against Sebi chief with Lokpal

https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/rssfeedstopstories.cms
2024-09-13 06:09:07.771962+00:00
Trinamool Congress MP Mahua Moitra has filed a complaint with the Lokpal against SEBI Chairperson Madhabi Puri Buch, alleging improper conduct and quid pro quo arrangements. The complaint highlights Buch's alleged misconduct during her tenure at SEBI and raises concerns about her financial ties with ICICI Bank and Greater Pacific Capital, urging an investigation under the Prevention of Corruption Act.
