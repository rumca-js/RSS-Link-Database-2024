[1136721] Japan scrambles jets as Russian aircraft circle country

https://www.lemonde.fr/en/rss/une.xml
2024-09-13 04:19:29.913382+00:00
The last time Russian military aircraft circled Japan was in 2019. Earlier this week, Russian and Chinese warships began joint drills in the Sea of Japan. The drills are part of a major naval exercise that Russian President Vladimir Putin has described as the largest of its kind in three decades.
