[1138701] Teenager who appeared in Flintoff’s Field of Dreams killed in car crash

https://www.theguardian.com/world/rss
2024-09-13 11:51:42+00:00
Umar Mahmood, 18, died alongside Adam Bodi, 16, his former schoolmate, when car hit trees in Preston
A teenager who appeared in the TV programme Freddie Flintoff’s Field of Dreams has been killed in a car crash.
Umar Mahmood, 18, who featured in the first series of the BBC show in 2022, being coached by the former England cricket all-rounder Andrew “Freddie” Flintoff, died alongside 16-year-old Adam Bodi.
