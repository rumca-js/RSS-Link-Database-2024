[1137868] China set to raise retirement age as demographic crisis looms

https://www.lemonde.fr/en/rss/une.xml
2024-09-13 09:46:08.044895+00:00
China's retirement age has been among the lowest in the world. The plan announced on Friday is to gradually raise the age for male workers from 60 to 63 and for female workers from 55 to 58.
