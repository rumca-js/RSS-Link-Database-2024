[1137184] Karnataka's night of chaos: Mob plunders 25 shops, sets stolen vehicles on fire

https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/rssfeedstopstories.cms
2024-09-13 07:14:08.693138+00:00
In Nagamangala, looters targeted over 25 shops, setting properties and stolen motorcycles ablaze. CCTV footage showed extensive vandalism. Firefighters battled the flames into early morning hours. Approximately 10 FIRs have been filed. Vishwa Hindu Parishad and Bajrang Dal announced a bandh in response to the violence on Friday.
