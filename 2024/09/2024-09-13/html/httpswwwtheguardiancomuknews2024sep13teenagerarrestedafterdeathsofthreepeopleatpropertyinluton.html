[1139805] Teenager arrested after deaths of three people at Luton tower block

https://www.theguardian.com/rss
2024-09-13 13:40:06+00:00
Man, 18, held after emergency services find three people with injuries who were pronounced dead at scene
A teenager has been arrested on suspicion of murder after the deaths of three people at a property in Luton on Friday morning.
Officers were called at about 5.30am amid concerns for the occupants of a flat in Leabank. Emergency services attended and discovered three people with injuries; all three were pronounced dead at the scene.
