[1137660] Delhi anti-Sikh riots case: Cong leader Jagdish Tytler pleads not guilty

https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/rssfeedstopstories.cms
2024-09-13 09:24:25.477749+00:00
Former union minister Jagdish Tytler has denied charges related to the killing of three people during the 1984 anti-Sikh riots in Delhi. The court has listed the matter for trial on October 3, following an order to frame charges against him. The court noted that Tytler incited the mob violence.
