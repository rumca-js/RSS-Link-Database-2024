[1138430] Former Met officer jailed for stealing from man who died after collapsing

https://www.theguardian.com/world/rss
2024-09-13 11:01:56+00:00
Craig Carter sentenced to 16 months after admitting stealing £115 from the Italian filmmaker Claudio Gaetani
A former Metropolitan police officer has been jailed for 16 months after he admitted stealing money from a man who died after collapsing.
Craig Carter, of Harlow, Essex, stole about £115 from the Italian film-maker and actor Claudio Gaetani between 7 and 14 September 2022, after Gaetani collapsed and died on a London street. He was visiting at the time.
