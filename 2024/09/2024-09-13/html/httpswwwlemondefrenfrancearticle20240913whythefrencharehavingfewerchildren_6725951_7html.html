[1140877] Why the French are having fewer children

https://www.lemonde.fr/en/rss/une.xml
2024-09-13 17:40:24.604779+00:00
France's fertility rate, which measures the number of children per woman, has fallen from 2.03 in 2010 to 1.68 in 2023, below the threshold of 2.1 needed to sustain population replacement. Family policies have not reversed this trend.
