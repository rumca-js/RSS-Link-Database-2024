[1139934] Biden Takes Aim at China's Temu and Shein with Trade Crackdown

https://feeds.a.dj.com/rss/RSSWorldNews.xml
2024-09-13 10:14:00+00:00
The Biden administration will restrict the use of a trade provision that lets China-founded e-commerce giants such as Temu and Shein to more easily ship to the U.S., a move that comes amid a groundswell of bipartisan pressure to close what critics regard as a “loophole.”
