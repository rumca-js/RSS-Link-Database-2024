[1139237] Hubble captures breathtaking images of Red Spider Nebula, 3,000 light-years away

https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/rssfeedstopstories.cms
2024-09-13 14:57:26.114794+00:00
NASA's Hubble Space Telescope has once again amazed astronomers with breathtaking images of the Red Spider Nebula, located 3,000 light-years away in Sagittarius. Featuring shockwaves and spider-leg-like arcs created by one of the hottest stars in the universe, these images have captivated social media users and highlight Hubble's enduring legacy in space exploration.
