The US has imposed sanctions on five entities and one individual involved in proliferating ballistic missiles and controlled missile technology. Among those targeted is the Beijing Research Institute of Automation for Machine Building Industry. These actions aim to prevent the spread of weapons of mass destruction and their delivery systems.
