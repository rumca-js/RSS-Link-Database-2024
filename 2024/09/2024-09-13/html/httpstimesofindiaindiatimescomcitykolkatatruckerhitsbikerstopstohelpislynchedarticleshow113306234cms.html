[1136628] Trucker hits biker, stops to help, is lynched

https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/rssfeedstopstories.cms
2024-09-13 03:59:21.254247+00:00
A truck driver from Maharashtra was killed by locals in Kharagpur, West Bengal, after stopping to help a biker he had accidentally hit. The biker also died in the incident. Police intervened to control the situation and rescue the driver, resulting in injuries to seven officers during the scuffle.
