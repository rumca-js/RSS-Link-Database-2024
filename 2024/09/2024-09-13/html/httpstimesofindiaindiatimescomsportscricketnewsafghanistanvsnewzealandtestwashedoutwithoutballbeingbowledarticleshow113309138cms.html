[1136621] Afghan vs NZ Test washed out without ball being bowled at Greater Noida

https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/rssfeedstopstories.cms
2024-09-13 03:59:07.108105+00:00
The one-off Test between Afghanistan and New Zealand was officially abandoned after five days of continuous rain at Greater Noida. This marks just the eighth instance in Test cricket history where a match has been called off without a single ball being bowled. Persistent rainfall prevented even the toss from taking place.
