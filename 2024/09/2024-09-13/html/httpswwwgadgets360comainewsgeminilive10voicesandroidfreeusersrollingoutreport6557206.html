[1138741] Gemini Live With Multiple Voices Reportedly Rolling Out to Free Gemini Users on Android

https://feeds.feedburner.com/gadgets360-latest
2024-09-13 12:58:33.782907+00:00
Gemini Live, the two-way conversational artificial intelligence (AI) feature by Google, is reportedly rolling out to Gemini free users on Android. The voice mode was first unveiled during Google I/O in May, and was rolled out to Gemini Advanced users last week. However, a new report claims that the feature is now being expanded to all users of Gemini on Android.
