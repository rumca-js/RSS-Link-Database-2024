Prime Minister Narendra Modi praised India's Paralympic team for their outstanding performance at the Paris Games, where they secured a record-breaking 29 medals. The haul included seven golds, nine silvers, and 13 bronze medals. Highlights included Avani Lekhara's two golds in shooting and Sumit Antil's record-breaking javelin throw. India finished in 18th place overall.
