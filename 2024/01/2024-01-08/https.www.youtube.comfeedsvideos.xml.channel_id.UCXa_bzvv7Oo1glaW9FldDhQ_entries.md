With each new game that Remedy Entertainment has put out, its stock has risen higher and higher, to the point that it’s now considered to be among the most highly talented studios in the games industry. And amongst all of its many accomplishments, one that continues to stand out even now, more than two decades after it first came out, is the original Max Payne. 

Though parts of the experience have certainly aged – as is inevitable for a game this old – its biggest strengths continue to shine to this day, from its hardboiled detective story to its slick neo-noir aesthetic, from how convincingly it brought its unique vision to life to, of course, the incredible bullet time mechanic. Here, we talk about all of that and more.
