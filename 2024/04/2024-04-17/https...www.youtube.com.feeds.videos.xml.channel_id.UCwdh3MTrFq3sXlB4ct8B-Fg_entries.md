Adding weathering or chipping to your models can be a great way to take them up a notch. Whether it’s an extra finishing touch or a stylistic choice that appears across your entire army, hard-worn gear is super fun to paint – and can be surprisingly easy, too! In this video, Ollie will be showing you just a few ways you can start weathering models from your collection. From simple armour chipping to rust and verdigris, these techniques will work on just about everything.
