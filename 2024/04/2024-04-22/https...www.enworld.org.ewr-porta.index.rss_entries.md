<div class="bbWrapper">Action-Heroes is a cinematic toolkit role-playing game with a D6 dice pool system. The game encourages collaborative play in a group that give players the tools to play characters that are larger than life doing explosive action that roots them as the center of their world.<br />
<br />
There are no classes in the <b><i>Action-Heroes</i></b> RPG, and characters are built using pools of points that allow players to customize their characters to the ideas that they have in their heads.<br />
