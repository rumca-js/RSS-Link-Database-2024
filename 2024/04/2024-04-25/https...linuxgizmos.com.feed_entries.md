## ADLINK Launches Innovative A380E Graphics Card Featuring Intel Arc GPU
 - [https://linuxgizmos.com/adlink-launches-innovative-a380e-graphics-card-featuring-intel-arc-gpu](https://linuxgizmos.com/adlink-launches-innovative-a380e-graphics-card-featuring-intel-arc-gpu)
 - RSS feed: https://linuxgizmos.com/feed
 - date published: 2024-04-25T06:40:47+00:00

ADLINK Technology Inc. has unveiled the EGX-PCIE-A380E graphics card, integrated with the Intel Arc A380E GPU. This new card is specifically designed to enhance commercial gaming and edge AI applications, supported by compatibility with the OpenVino toolkit. The card leverages the Arc Alchemist architecture and is equipped with 6GB of GDDR6 memory, ensuring high efficiency [&#8230;]
