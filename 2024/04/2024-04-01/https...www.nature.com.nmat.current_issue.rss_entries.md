## Heterostructures coupling ultrathin metal carbides and chalcogenides
 - [https://www.nature.com/articles/s41563-024-01827-x](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41563-024-01827-x)
 - RSS feed: https://www.nature.com/nmat/current_issue/rss

<p>Nature Materials, Published online: 01 April 2024; <a href="https://www.nature.com/articles/s41563-024-01827-x">doi:10.1038/s41563-024-01827-x</a></p>Non-layered transition metal carbides (TMCs) and layered transition metal dichalcogenides (TMDs) can form various heterostructure configurations through chemical conversion. This Review highlights the progress in the fabrication and control of TMC/TMD heterostructures and the exotic properties arising from these interfaces.
