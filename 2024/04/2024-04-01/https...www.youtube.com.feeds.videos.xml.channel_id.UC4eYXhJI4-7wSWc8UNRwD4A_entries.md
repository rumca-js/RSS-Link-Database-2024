In 2021 the Tiny Desk x globalFEST series was created to promote international music discovery. For the last three years, artists from around the globe have performed Tiny Desk (home) concerts from their respective countries, but for the first time this year we invited artists to perform at the Tiny Desk in NPR's headquarters.

Suraya Mohamed | April 1, 2024
