<p><span>

With my </span>

<a href="https://www.jefftk.com/news/emandoharp">electronic harp mandolin</a>

hardware.  And a few weeks ago, after reading about Ugo Conti's 

<a href="https://www.jefftk.com/p/ugo-contis-whistle-controlled-synthesizer">whistle-controlled

<a href="https://www.jefftk.com/whistle-synth">my whistle synth</a> [1] is bulky and
expensive.  Which has me excited about a new project: I'd like to make
an embedded version.
