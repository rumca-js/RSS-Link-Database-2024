## A DNA origami device spatially controls CD95 signalling to induce immune tolerance in rheumatoid arthritis
 - [https://www.nature.com/articles/s41563-024-01865-5](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41563-024-01865-5)
 - RSS feed: https://www.nature.com/nmat/current_issue/rss
 - date published: 2024-04-09T10:05:24.929437+00:00

<p>Nature Materials, Published online: 09 April 2024; <a href="https://www.nature.com/articles/s41563-024-01865-5">doi:10.1038/s41563-024-01865-5</a></p>A pH-responsive DNA origami device displays a precise geometric array of CD95 ligands to selectively induce activated immune cell death and elicit localized immune tolerance to alleviate rheumatoid arthritis.
