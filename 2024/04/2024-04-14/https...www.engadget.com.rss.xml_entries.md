## Apple claims Epic is trying to ‘micromanage’ its business operations in a new court filing

<p>Last month, Epic Games filed a motion asking a California judge to hold Apple in contempt for what it claims are violations of a 2021 injunction relating to the company’s App Store practices. Now, Apple is asking the judge to reject Epic’s request, alleging in a new filing spotted by <a class="no-affiliate-link" href="https://www.reuters.com/legal/apple-denies-violating-us-court-order-epic-games-lawsuit-2024-04-13/?taid=661aca7c6ed467000111773e"><em><ins>Reuters</ins></em></a> that the motion is an attempt to “micromanage Apple’s business operations in a way that would increase Epic’s profitability.”</p>
