## GStreamer 1.24 Multimedia Framework Released With Vulkan H.264 & H.265 Video Decoders
 - [https://www.linuxtoday.com/news/gstreamer-1-24-multimedia-framework-released-with-vulkan-h-264-h-265-video-decoders](https://www.linuxtoday.com/news/gstreamer-1-24-multimedia-framework-released-with-vulkan-h-264-h-265-video-decoders)
 - RSS feed: https://www.linuxtoday.com/news/feed
 - date published: 2024-03-05T15:00:01+00:00

<p>Highlights of the GStreamer 1.24 release include Vulkan H.264 and H.265 video decoders, a new unixfd plugin for efficient 1:N inter-process communication on Linux, new GstMeta for SMPTE ST-291M HANC/VANC Ancillary Data, a new W3C Media Source Extensions library, and DRM Modifier Support for dmabufs on Linux.</p>
