It’s no secret that we have been seeing a resurgence of remakes of classic games in the last few years, and while there is an argument to be had that developers should focus on building new IPs rather than remaking old games, it’s also inarguable that these remakes provide an avenue for new  players to discover a classic without having to go through the many frustrations that come along with playing the original. 

The PS3 generation is home to plenty of games that would benefit a lot from a ground-up remake that’s built for PS5. In this video, we will be taking a look at 15 such games ranging from shooters like Resistance: Fall of Man to action adventures like Enslaved and much more.
