<p>Lair of the Crystal Fang is another entry in the excellent series pulpy novels based on the board game adapted from Fantasy Flight games&#8217; Arkham Horror as well as the works of H.P. Lovecraft. It is the third of the &#8220;Andy Van Notwick&#8221; books by S.A. Sidor. The Last Ritual and The Cult of the Spider Queen loosely follow cub reporter Andy Van Notwick in his attempt to find the big scoop that will propel him to fame as an investigative journalist. These stories inevitably take him into Arkham, Massachusetts’s seedy occult underbelly and are unprintable by the end. Lair of the Crystal Fang deals with Andy having gotten his employment terminated after his attempt to sell his story with insufficient evidence. He&#8217;s now reduced to cleaning up dirty apartments in the worst part of the city.
