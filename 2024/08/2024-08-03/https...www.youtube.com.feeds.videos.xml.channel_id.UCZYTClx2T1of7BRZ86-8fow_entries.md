## A Better Treatment for Overdose Is Coming
 - [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yzqvBW2eHZQ](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yzqvBW2eHZQ)
 - RSS feed: https://www.youtube.com/feeds/videos.xml?channel_id=UCZYTClx2T1of7BRZ86-8fow
 - date published: 2024-08-03T17:00:42+00:00

Naloxone, better known as Narcan, is today's gold standard to treat opioid overdose. But sometimes it comes up short against more potent opioids like fentanyl. So future treatments might take advantage of opioid receptor agonist/antagonist combos, antibodies, or nanoparticles!
