## REVIEW: The Dissonance by Shaun Hamill

<p>The Dissonance is the latest contemporary fantasy novel by author Shaun Hamill. Hamill may be known to readers for his novel writing with A Cosmology of Monsters or his short fiction, which includes writing in the Conan universe. The Dissonance is the first of his works I have read, and overall, I have enjoyed reading it. However, as a contemporary fantasy (although it deals with some very dark topics and events), it is a departure from many of my usual reading choices. Hamill&#8217;s combination of horror and fantasy worked well for me, similar to Leigh Bardugo&#8217;s Ninth House or Stephen King&#8217;s The Dark Tower series, and has many of the features a fan of darker fantasy would enjoy. Described as an &#8220;epic contemporary fantasy,&#8221; The Dissonance is the story of four unlikely friends, Hal, Athena, Erin, and Peter, their accidental discovery of magic, and the consequences of their terrible mistakes.
