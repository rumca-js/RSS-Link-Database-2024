After all, how else can you explain the number of video games featuring one or both roles? If it isn't a beat 'em up like The Ninja Saviors: Return of the Warriors, then it's a precision platformer like N++. Of course, recent years have provided open-world action RPGs like Rise of the Ronin catering to those who live by the blade.

Meanwhile, Ghostrunner 2 captures that fantasy of effortlessly slicing through hordes of enemies while dancing between life and death. Check out our picks for 15 compelling samurai and ninja games and what makes them worth playing.
