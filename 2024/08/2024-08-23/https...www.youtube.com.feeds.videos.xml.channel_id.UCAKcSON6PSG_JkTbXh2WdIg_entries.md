Ahead of their sold-out show at Surly Brewing Festival Field on June 20, the Pixies stopped by The Current studio to play a three-song set of songs from their upcoming album The Night the Zombies Came, out October 25 via BMG. The new album was produced by Tom Dalgety and is the band’s first with with new bassist Emma Richardson, who joined the group after the departure of Paz Lenchantin.
