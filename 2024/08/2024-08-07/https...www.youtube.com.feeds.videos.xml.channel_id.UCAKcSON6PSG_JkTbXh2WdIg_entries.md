Bon Iver covering "Battle Cry of Freedom," a patriotic song written in 1862, at the rally for Vice President and Democratic presidential nominee Kamala Harris and her running mate, Minnesota Gov. Tim Walz, on Wednesday, Aug. 7, 2024, in Eau Claire, Wisconsin.

"I love this melody and I love y’all," said Bon Iver's Justin Vernon. "I appreciate you."
