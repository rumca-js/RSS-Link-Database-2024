## Predesigned perovskite crystal waveguides for room-temperature exciton–polariton condensation and edge lasing
 - [https://www.nature.com/articles/s41563-024-01980-3](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41563-024-01980-3)
 - RSS feed: https://www.nature.com/nmat/current_issue/rss
 - date published: 2024-08-19T09:37:24.192709+00:00

<p>Nature Materials, Published online: 19 August 2024; <a href="https://www.nature.com/articles/s41563-024-01980-3">doi:10.1038/s41563-024-01980-3</a></p>A method to fabricate arbitrarily shaped perovskite crystals is measured, apt for the realization of integrated photonic circuitry, demonstrating room-temperature waveguided exciton–polariton condensation and bright polariton edge lasing.
