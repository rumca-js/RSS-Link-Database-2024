## SpacemiT Muse Pi: A RISC-V SBC Featuring the SpacemiT M1 SoC
 - [https://linuxgizmos.com/spacemit-muse-pi-a-risc-v-sbc-featuring-the-spacemit-m1-soc](https://linuxgizmos.com/spacemit-muse-pi-a-risc-v-sbc-featuring-the-spacemit-m1-soc)

The SpacemiT Muse Pi is a development board that leverages the cutting-edge RISC-V architecture. It is powered by the SpacemiT M1, an Octa-core System-on-Chip, which is designed to support multiple serial peripherals and a range of wireless communication standards. In terms of design, the Muse Pi maintains a similar form factor to the Banana Pi [&#8230;]
