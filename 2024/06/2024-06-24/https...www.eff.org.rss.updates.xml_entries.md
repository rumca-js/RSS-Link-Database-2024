## EFF Opposes the American Privacy Rights Act

<div class="field field--name-body field--type-text-with-summary field--label-hidden"><div class="field__items"><div class="field__item even"><p>Protecting people's privacy is the <a href="https://www.eff.org/wp/privacy-first-better-way-address-online-harms">first step</a> we should take to create meaningful online regulation.
