After delivering one of the best games of 2023 in Alan Wake 2, Remedy Entertainment is now back with more new content for the fans of the long awaited survival horror sequel in the form of Night Springs, the first of the game’s two planned post launch expansions. 

Split across three episodes that each tell a “what if?” story centered around a different character, Night Springs is built as a fun, quirky side adventure for fans to dive into, which obviously means that it’s not exactly an essential part of the Alan Wake 2 experience.
