## Obesity treatment is undergoing a revolution
 - [https://www.lemonde.fr/en/science/article/2024/06/21/obesity-treatment-is-undergoing-a-revolution_6675403_10.html](https://www.lemonde.fr/en/science/article/2024/06/21/obesity-treatment-is-undergoing-a-revolution_6675403_10.html)
 - RSS feed: https://www.lemonde.fr/en/science/rss_full.xml
 - date published: 2024-06-21T18:30:16+00:00

Molecules that mimic digestive hormones and act on satiety are revolutionizing the treatment of obese patients. French health authorities have been slow to approve their marketing, while, elsewhere, there is a rush to buy them, even though their long-term effects remain unknown.
