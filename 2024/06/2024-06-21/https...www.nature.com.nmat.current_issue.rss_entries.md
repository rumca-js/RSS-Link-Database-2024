## Damage-free dry transfer method using stress engineering for high-performance flexible two- and three-dimensional electronics

 - RSS feed: https://www.nature.com/nmat/current_issue/rss

<p>Nature Materials, Published online: 21 June 2024; <a href="https://www.nature.com/articles/s41563-024-01931-y">doi:10.1038/s41563-024-01931-y</a></p>Current transfer printing technologies enable versatile flexible devices but challenges remain. Here the authors report a facile, versatile and damage-free dry transfer printing strategy based on stress control of the deposited thin films.
