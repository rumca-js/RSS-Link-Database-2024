More and more with each passing day, the games industry is growing increasingly obsessed with delivering large scale, never-ending experiences that players can clock hundreds of hours into, and as the market becomes saturated with them, the value of relatively smaller and more focused experiences becomes clearer and clearer. 

That said, games that last 15-20 hours and manage to squeeze out everything they can out of every minute in that runtime are, thankfully enough, not exactly a rarity either, even if they are not as popular as ginormous 100 hour epics, and here, that is exactly what we will be focusing on. Here are a few 20 hour solo games from recent years that you absolutely should now miss out on.
