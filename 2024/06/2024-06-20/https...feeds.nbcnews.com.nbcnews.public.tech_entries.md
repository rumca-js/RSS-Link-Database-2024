## Whistleblower says Airbnb weakened its policies on extremists and hate groups
 - [https://www.nbcnews.com/now/video/whistleblower-says-airbnb-weakened-its-policies-on-extremists-and-hate-groups-213315141579](https://www.nbcnews.com/now/video/whistleblower-says-airbnb-weakened-its-policies-on-extremists-and-hate-groups-213315141579)
 - RSS feed: https://feeds.nbcnews.com/nbcnews/public/tech
 - date published: 2024-06-20T01:16:59+00:00

Airbnb weakened its policies against extremists and hate groups last year and dissolved its team tasked with removing them from the platform, a former investigations analyst for the company alleged in a recent whistleblower complaint. NBC News' Brandy Zadrozny interviews the whistleblower and reports on what Airbnb is saying in response.
