<p>Nature Materials, Published online: 18 June 2024; <a href="https://www.nature.com/articles/s41563-024-01913-0">doi:10.1038/s41563-024-01913-0</a></p>Heat in electronic devices is normally dissipated via cooling. Here the authors engineer the thermal dynamics of the Mott transition and dynamical thermal interactions with the substrate to enable neuromorphic computing in a NbOx-based device.
