EvilVEvil is a 3-player co-op shooter that puts players in the shoes of vampires that have been awoken after a deep slumber in the year 2099. 
Players must go through 12 missions as they shoot, blow up, and feed their way through hordes of enemies, both humans and demons, as they take on this cult. 

Ultimately, EvilVEvil ends up feeling like a relatively bland game. Its visuals are dull, and its progression systems are quite shallow, with poor choices like locking weapon unlocks behind individual character progression.
