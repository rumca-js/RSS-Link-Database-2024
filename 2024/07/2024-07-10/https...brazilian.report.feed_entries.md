<p>The Senate Foreign Affairs Committee on Wednesday approved the nominations for Brazil’s new ambassadors to Mexico, Ukraine, Singapore, and four other countries. Senators also approved the nominations for the upcoming ambassadors to Angola, Cabo Verde, Estonia, and Saint Lucia. The nominations are now pending confirmation by the Senate floor.
