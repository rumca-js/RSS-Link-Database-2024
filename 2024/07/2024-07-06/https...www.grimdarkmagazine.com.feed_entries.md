<p>The Hidden Queen by Peter V. Brett The Hidden Queen is the second instalment in Peter V. Brett&#8217;s epic fantasy series, The Nightfall Saga, was released in March 2024. I picked up The Hidden Queen immediately after reading the first book in the series, The Desert Prince, which I was thoroughly impressed with. When picking up The Hidden Queen, I was almost sure I would enjoy it, and I did enjoy it immensely. Brett&#8217;s captivating writing is the sort of great fantasy we should all be able to find pleasure in. If epic fantasy series are your thing, you will enjoy your time reading Brett&#8217;s work. Before reading The Hidden Queen, you must have already read The Desert Prince, but you do not have to know about Brett&#8217;s Demon Cycle series, which is set in the same world. Although they complement each other very well, the two series are separate, and the earlier do not need to have been read as a foundation for the latter.
