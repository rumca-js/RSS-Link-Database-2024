How to Maximize the Benefits of Neurodiversity in Software Development Teams and Support Neurodivergent Developers - Dom Davis - ACCU 2024
---

These days it seems everyone has Autism, or ADHD, or AuDHD, or some other form of neurodivergence. But is this an explosion of self diagnosed folks who have watched too much TikTok, or a genuine realisation that there are a lot of us who are not neurotypical, and who view the world differently?

Turns out Software Development is an industry that fits well with many neurodivergent folks which means there are many of us about, sometimes hiding in plain sight.

In this session we'll look at what it is like to be neurodivergent in a neurotypical world, some strategies neurodivergent folks can employ, and how you can support and be an ally to those who are neurodivergent.
