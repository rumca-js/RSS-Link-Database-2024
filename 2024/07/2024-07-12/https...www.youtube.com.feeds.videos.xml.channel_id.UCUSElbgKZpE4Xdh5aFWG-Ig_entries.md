Today we will travel to the Salto el Hacha waterfall, located in Venezuela in the Canaima National Park.  Hacha Falls is a unique natural site of beauty. The waterfall keeps a secret. Behind the raging wall of water there is a tunnel, through which you can go right through the waterfall. You can’t see anything behind the splashes. You need to step into the unknown, but, having overcome the dense water flow, a wet cave is revealed, overgrown with vines at the entrance.
