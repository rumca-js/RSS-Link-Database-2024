 - date published: 2024-07-23T16:49:53+00:00

<p>This started out as a weekend hack with gpt-4-mini, using the very basic strategy of "just ask the ai to ocr the document".<p>But this turned out to be better performing than our current implementation of Unstructured/Textract.
