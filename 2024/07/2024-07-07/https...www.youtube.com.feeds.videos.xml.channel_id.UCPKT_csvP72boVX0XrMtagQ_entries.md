☞ About Disclosure

Disclosure, the dance duo of brothers Guy and Howard Lawrence, have been operating in the UK since 2010. Their deep house sound is tinged with UK garage and dancehall influences, and the duo regularly work with some of the biggest names in recent pop music, including Sam Smith, Flume, Lorde and The Weeknd.

☞ About Mochakk

Rising up out of Brazil’s hotbed of formidable talent, Mochakk (Pedro Maia) is a meticulate producer and performer of house music, who infuses his art with traditional Brazilian music and skate and hip-hop culture.
