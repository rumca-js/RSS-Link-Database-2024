## Inulin-gel-based oral immunotherapy remodels the small intestinal microbiome and suppresses food allergy
 - [https://www.nature.com/articles/s41563-024-01909-w](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41563-024-01909-w)
 - RSS feed: https://www.nature.com/nmat/current_issue/rss
 - date published: 2024-07-08T10:21:59.021455+00:00

<p>Nature Materials, Published online: 08 July 2024; <a href="https://www.nature.com/articles/s41563-024-01909-w">doi:10.1038/s41563-024-01909-w</a></p>An oral inulin allergen gel restores the microbiota of allergic mice and suppresses undesired immune responses to achieve allergen-specific oral tolerance, effectively overcoming food allergy.
