On April 26, 2024, Iron & Wine, aka Sam Beam, released his seventh full-length studio album, "Light Verse," on @subpop. In a bit of a departure from his previous works, Beam recorded this album in Los Angeles with producer Dave Way.

The album itself presents Iron & Wine in a creative, playful space. While on tour in support of the record, Beam visited The Current studio to perform solo versions of some songs from Light Verse. Afterwards, he spent some time visiting and sharing a few laughs with host Mac Wilson. Watch that interview above.
