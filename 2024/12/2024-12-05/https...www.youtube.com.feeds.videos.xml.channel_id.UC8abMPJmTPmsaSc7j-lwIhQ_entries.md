Produced by the legendary Florenz Ziegfeld, the film captures the glamour and extravagance of the Ziegfeld Follies, while also hinting at the darker realities behind the dazzling spectacle.   

The film follows the journey of Gloria Hughes (Mary Eaton), a small-town girl who dreams of becoming a Ziegfeld girl. Through a series of auditions and rehearsals, Gloria navigates the competitive world of show business, encountering both supportive mentors and opportunistic figures.
