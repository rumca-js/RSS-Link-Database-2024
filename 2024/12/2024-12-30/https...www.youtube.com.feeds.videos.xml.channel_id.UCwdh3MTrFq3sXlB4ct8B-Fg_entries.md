These merciless warriors are keen infiltrators, and sneaking up on their unsuspecting foes requires a subtle camouflage to get the job done. So obviously, a mission such as this deserves the brightest greens and yellows! 
In this video, Dan goes through how to paint a Striking Scorpion using contrast paints to lay down the bright colours needed for such sneaky tasks!
