dance</a> that requires high filtration masks (<a href="https://blog.bidadance.org/2022/05/requiring-high-filtration-masks.html">N95
etc</a>) at half of our dances.  When we <a href="https://blog.bidadance.org/2022/01/getting-back-to-dancing.html">restarted</a>
in 2022 we required masks at all our dances, before <a href="https://blog.bidadance.org/2023/06/some-mask-optional-dances.html">switching
to half</a> in 2023.  We just <a href="https://blog.bidadance.org/2023/06/some-mask-optional-dances.html">ran
a survey</a> of our dancers, and while there are people who would like
to not have to wear masks there are also a lot of people who are only
willing to come if they know all the dancers will be masked. [1]
