 - RSS feed: $source

								<p>The earliest version of Orcs in <strong>DnD</strong> was a simple, violent threat to civilization that needed to be pacified by brave adventurers, which unfortunately echoed stereotypes used against tribal and indigenous human cultures. The latest version of the Player’s Handbook gives Orcs a sunnier disposition, but this only makes it more obvious how much the core fantasy of DnD relies on the existence of uncomplicated ‘baddies’ that players can bash without remorse.</p>
