Maya de Vitry describes how she came to play a Blueridge BR-143 and why it’s the guitar most suitable for her songwriting, performing and touring.

Here's what de Vitry had to say:

"I'm Maya de Vitry, and I play a Blueridge guitar, and the model is a BR-143. I got this guitar in 2017, and I ordered it off the internet. All almost every song that I've written since has been written on this guitar.

"My former bandmate, Oliver Craven — who was with me in the Stray Birds, the trio I used to play in — he was in touch with Blueridge about an artist, some kind of artist deal or artist discount.
