Glass mosaic tips…some glass mosaics are difficult to install vertically. One option is to set them on a piece of KERDI or 1/4” backer board. This keeps all the mosaics in the same plane and makes the installation inside the niche much easier 👍🏽🔥#tile #glassmosaic #bathroomremodel #homeremodeling
