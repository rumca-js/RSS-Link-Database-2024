Intel has officially announced its next Arc GPUs under architecture codename "Battlemage," with the release date for the Intel Arc B580 GPU set for December 13, 2024 and the Arc B570 GPU set for January 16, 2025. The B580 and B570 GPUs have an MSRP baseline of $250 and $220, respectively, though add-in board partner models will set their own pricing. The B580 will include an Intel board, with B570 as partner only.
