<p>The Prince Without Sorrow is the debut novel by author Maithree Wijesekara. Its publisher, Harper Voyager, describes it as &#8221;the debut fantasy sensation of 2025&#8221; and says it is perfect for Hannah Kaner and Samantha Shannon fans. That was enough information to pick it up and dive into this new world. That and the giant leopards and flying serpents also piqued my interest. Set in the Ran Empire and inspired by Emperor Ashoka of the Mauryan Dynasty of Ancient India, The Prince Without Sorrow has two points of view that we follow. The first is the witch Shakti, a mayakari trained to commune with nature and do no harm. But the emperor has persecuted Shakti&#8217;s people for decades, burning mayakari alive (or worse) if he finds them. Choosing violence might be the only way Shakti can keep her people safe.
