								<p>Fans are confused and outraged by a new <b>Lego</b> set revealed on November 29. 76313 Marvel Logo is, as the name implies, a literal brick re-creation of the Marvel logo. It’s a dull, oblong structure, with its monotony only broken up by a few Marvel minifigs ‘leaping’ out from it thanks to some Technic features. Several Lego community members have taken to social media to lament this reveal in the week since it appeared.</p>
