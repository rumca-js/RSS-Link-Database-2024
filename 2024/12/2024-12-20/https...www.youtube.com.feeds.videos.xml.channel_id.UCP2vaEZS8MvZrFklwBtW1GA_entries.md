Bucket cleaning tip after setting tile…I let my bucket/water/mortar sit overnight. The next day I pour out the water. Then I remove the solids at the bottom with a bucket scoop. After rinsing the bucket it’s clean and ready to be used again 👍🏽🔥#tile #tilework #bathroomremodel #themoreyouknow
