Setup Notes:
The K.O.II is master clock, sending MIDI out via TRS cable to the Ottobit Jr. The filter sequence is set at infinity repeats, with 8x multiplier. Volca Keys is playing a single drone note, and is connected to K.O.II via sync (sync out on K.O.II with 8ths sync into Volca sync in). It's not entirely necessary as I wasn't using any motion sequencing (I could have, to make it even more textured and interesting). I wish I could have sent MIDI out to the volca as well as the Ottobit Jr at the same time, but I don't have a simple solution that doesn't require some big MIDI hub, lots of cables and extra power requirements. Maybe I'll figure something out - perhaps a headphone splitter might work?
