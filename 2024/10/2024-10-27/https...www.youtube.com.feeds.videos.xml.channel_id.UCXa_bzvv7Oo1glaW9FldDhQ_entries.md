## Why Microsoft Flight Simulator 2024 May Be the Biggest Game of the Year

We didn’t expect another Flight Simulator so soon after 2020’s excellent Microsoft Flight Simulator, but here we are – Microsoft Flight Simulator 2024 is expected on November 19th. 

Microsoft and Asobo Studio’s pledge that 2024’s offering will be “the most ambitious sim ever undertaken” might seem lofty, but there is an unfathomably large amount of content on offer in this latest iteration which aims to satisfy Flight Simulator’s broad audiences, from aviation and flight sim enthusiasts to digital tourists. 

Principal of this new content is a vast career mode, with numerous branching specialties covering all manner of aviation activities. An in-depth photography mode, covering a bucket list of wildlife, natural, and man-made wonders, will provide an enthralling way to engage with Flight Simulator 2024.
