<p>Once again, a dangerous social media trend has reared its head. The so-called “chroming” challenge involves sniffing nail polish remover, deodorants, adhesives, paint thinners, and other cleaning products while filming yourself for TikTok.</p>



<p>The official aim of chroming? To record yourself achieving a brief state of chemical intoxication, then posting the video online as if you’re part of some dubious competition, in the hopes of getting as many likes and expanding your reach as far as possible.</p>



<p>Some TikTokers do end up getting millions of views this way, but chroming has its risks — and they just aren’t worth it. The challenge has already claimed a few lives, including one 11-year-old boy in the UK as <a href="https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c724xpzwgzko">reported by the BCC</a>.</p>
