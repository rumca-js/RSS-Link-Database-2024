From the director of Persona 5, Metaphor: ReFantazio is an RPG that blends turn-based combat with real-time action, alongside social elements. Here is everything you need to know about the game. 
 
Metaphor takes place in the Kingdom of Euchronia, where the king has recently been assassinated. With no living heirs to succeed him, the kingdom has become a land of chaos and unrest. If only the people knew the prince was in fact still alive, just cursed. It is up to you and your fairy companion Gallica to lift the deadly curse off the prince, however in order to do that you must take part in a royal tournament for the throne that has broken out. In the daytime, You'll go on quests, dive into dungeons and discover various treasures. Night time is when you'll strengthen relationships and increase your virtues.
