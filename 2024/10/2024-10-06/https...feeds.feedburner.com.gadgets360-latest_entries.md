## Google Pixel 9 Pro Fold Review: Jack of All Trades
 - [https://www.gadgets360.com/mobiles/reviews/google-pixel-9-pro-fold-review-6714890](https://www.gadgets360.com/mobiles/reviews/google-pixel-9-pro-fold-review-6714890)

Google's Pixel 9 Pro Fold is not the most powerful or capable foldable out there in terms of hardware, even though our expectations from it (especially after a year-long wait) are sky-high. Since it comes from the makers of Android, it is also expected to set new standards and steer the foldable experience for other manufacturers in the right direction. While it does get most of it right, there are a few hiccups. And some of these might end up being dealbreakers for a select few.
