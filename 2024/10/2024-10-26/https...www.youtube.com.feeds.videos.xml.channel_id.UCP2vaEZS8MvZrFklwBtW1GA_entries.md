How to waterproof bathtub against KERDI-BOARD…I apply thin-set to the wall then apply KERDI-FIX against the tub flange. This seals the banding to the tub and the wall. One important tip, apply KERDI-FIX after the mortar because it will only remain tacky for 5-10 minutes.
