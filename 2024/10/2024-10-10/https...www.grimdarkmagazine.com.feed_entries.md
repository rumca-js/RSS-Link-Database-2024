<p>If grimdark is the home of the obscene, perhaps it’s no wonder that it draws in the outsider. Those deemed obscene by the wider world come to grimdark to express themselves without apology. It’s no secret to anyone who knows me that I’m ravenous for the resurgence of Western histfic and Western fantasy. One of my favourites thus far has been Amazon Prime’s miniseries The English (2022) starring Emily Blunt and Chaske Spencer. I was thrilled to watch (and rewatch) my beloved Western setting telling the story of a doomed Englishwoman and a stubborn Pawnee veteran struggling for survival and vengeance in the post-Civil War era. The English’s story—which takes place over six episodes—is gloriously violent, blood-soaked, mournful, determined, bleak, hopeful, and beautiful. An absolute smorgasbord for the grimdark fan, The English takes a hard, ugly stare at the history of the American West and how it treated its vulnerable.
