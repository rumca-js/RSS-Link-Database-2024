<p>Christopher Buehlman takes readers back a few decades from his first novel The Blacktongue Thief in this thrilling and dark prequel, The Daughter’s War. We follow Galva, known affectionately by her brothers as Galvicha, a soldier in an all-female warrior unit called the Knight Ravens. Accompanied by their corvids, large war-birds, created and magicked to fight against goblins, Galva treads through war-torn cities to protect other Kynd (humans) against their common enemy, often referred to as ‘Our Foe’. This is not a story on why and how a war has come about, nor is it about how to win this particular war. Instead, we follow the chronicle of one soldier’s experience, and how she managed to survive such a long and bloody campaign against an alien species that has whittled down civilisation immensely, to the point of her having to take up arms.
