## REVIEW: Sleepwalking by Daniel Barnett

<p>Well, I guess that’s it then… with Sleepwalking, Daniel Barnett has officially become a new favourite author and established The Nightmareland Chronicles as one of my all time favourites; this series is truly my horror dream (or should I say nightmare?) come true. If the previous instalment, Lullaby, was Mariah’s book, then Sleepwalking is Marcos’ book. Never before have I enjoyed a child character as much as I do Marcos, and it’s truly insane how deeply emotionally invested I have become in him in the short time since we first met him. He is somehow both extremely emotionally mature for his young age while also maintaining an endearing level of childhood innocence, which is exactly what makes him such a surprisingly complex and compelling kid.
