## NASA probe lifts off to discover whether Jupiter's icy moon has life

The Europa Clipper launched Monday to explore Jupiter's moon Europa and investigate whether its hidden ocean could support life. It will take 5.5 years to reach Jupiter, where it will orbit the planet and conduct multiple flybys.
