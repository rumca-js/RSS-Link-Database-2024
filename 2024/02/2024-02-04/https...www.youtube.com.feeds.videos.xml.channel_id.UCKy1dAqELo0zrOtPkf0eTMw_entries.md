Journey Beyond the Edge of the World is a first-person retro adventure exploration game developed by Markus Koepke. Players embody a sailor on a decommissioned fishing trawler leaving the port of Hamburg, Northern Germany in the year 1953. The player begins to experience a range of surreal events that eventually unfold into a journey to uncover the true intentions behind the trip and into the sailor's troubled mind. Journey Beyond the Edge of the World is releasing soon for PC.
