Killer Mike’s full-length solo release “Michael” recently won the Grammy Award for Best Rap Album (bringing Killer Mike's total number of Grammys to four). When Killer Mike visited The Current in July 2023, he talked with host Jill Riley about the meaning of his album, "Michael."

You can watch and read a complete transcript of the interview at: https://www.thecurrent.org/feature/2023/08/16/killer-mike-shares-his-music-and-his-story-michael-album
